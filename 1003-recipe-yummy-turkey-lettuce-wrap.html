Ingredients of Turkey Lettuce Wrap

  1. You need 1 lb of ground turkey.
  2. Prepare 1 tbs of olive oil.
  3. Prepare 2 cloves of minced garlic.
  4. It's of Small diced onion.
  5. You need 1/4 cup of hoisin sauce.
  6. Prepare 2 tbs of soy sauce.
  7. Prepare 1 tbs of rice vinegar.
  8. It's 1 tbs of ginger.
  9. It's 1 can of water chestnuts diced.
  10. It's to taste of Salt & Pepper.
