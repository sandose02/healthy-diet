3 bean salad step by step

  1. prepare soybeans according. to pkg..
  2. drain and rinse in cold water.
  3. combine beans, onion, cilantro in large bowl.
  4. whisk lime peel, juice, oil and pinch of salt.
  5. pour over beans and toss to coat.
  6. Cover and refrigerate up to 24 hours.
  7. Stir well before serving.
