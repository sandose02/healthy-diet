Ingredients of Healthy Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough

  1. Prepare 2 tbsp of natural peanut butter.
  2. It's 1/2 of scoop of vanilla protein powder.
  3. It's 1 tbsp of milk.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of chocolate chips (i used milk but dark is better).

Healthy Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough step by step

  1. Heat peanut butter in the microwave about 20 seconds so its soft.
  2. Add in protein powder and milk and stir. It should be a coarse meal..
  3. Add in chocolate chips. Enjoy!.
