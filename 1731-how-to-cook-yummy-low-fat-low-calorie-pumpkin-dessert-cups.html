Ingredients of Low fat-low calorie pumpkin dessert cups

  1. Prepare 3/4 cup of Splenda Sugar Blend.
  2. You need 2 tsp of Cinnamon.
  3. It's 1 tsp of Salt.
  4. You need 3/4 cup of Egg Substitute.
  5. You need 1 can (29 oz) of 100% Pure Pumpkin.
  6. It's 2 can of Carnation evaporated Fat Free Milk.

Low fat-low calorie pumpkin dessert cups instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Mix sugar blend, salt, cinnamon in a small bowl. Mix pumpkin and egg substitute and add to sugar mixture. Gradually stir in evaporated milk.
