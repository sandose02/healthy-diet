Ingredients of Amy's Healthy Carob Chip Cookies

  1. It's 1/2 cup of Sugar.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of Brown sugar.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of Butter.
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of Veggie oil.
  5. It's 1 of unit Egg.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of Vanilla.
  7. It's 1 tsp of Baking powder.
  8. Prepare 1 dash of Kosher salt.
  9. It's 1 1/2 cup of Whole wheat flour.
  10. It's 1/2 cup of Whole wheat pastry flour.
  11. It's 1/3 cup of Oats.
  12. It's 1 cup of Carob chips.
  13. It's 1/4 cup of Dried cranberries.

Amy's Healthy Carob Chip Cookies step by step

  1. Add softened butter, oil and sugars into a large bowl and mix until creamed. Then add vanilla and egg. Mix, do not over beat..
  2. Add all dry ingredients in a separate bowl. (Preheat oven to 375*).
  3. Add dry ingredients to the wet mixture in 1/2 cup increments..
  4. Add the carob and cranberries last..
  5. Place spoonful sized balls in rows on cookie sheet..
  6. Bake for 8-12 minutes, depending on the size and amount..
  7. Let cool and enjoy!!! *it is easy to sub whole wheat flour for white, carob chips for chocolate, cranberries for your favorite dried fruit, add cinnamon, nutmeg, or any other spice. Coconut flakes are amazing when added. Get creative!.
