Ingredients of Yummy Diet Greek Salad

  1. Prepare 150 g of low-fat feta cheese, cubed.
  2. It's 1 of lettuce head, cut into small pieces.
  3. You need 4 of medium tomatoes, cubed.
  4. You need 4 of medium cucumbers, sliced.
  5. It's 1 of small red onion, cut into rings.
  6. It's 1 of green bell peppers, cut into thin rings (optional).
  7. Prepare 10 of black olives, pits removed.
  8. Prepare of mint leaves optional.
  9. You need of Dressing:.
  10. It's 3 tablespoons of olive oil.
  11. Prepare 1 tablespoon of lemon juice.
  12. It's 1 tablespoon of red vinegar.
  13. It's 1 teaspoon of dried oregano.
  14. You need of salt to taste.

Yummy Diet Greek Salad instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix the dressing ingredients together until well combined..
  2. Place all the salad ingredients, except the black olives and feta cheese, in a large bowl and pour the dressing over them..
  3. Toss gently to combine. Arrange the olives and feta cheese on top and serve..
