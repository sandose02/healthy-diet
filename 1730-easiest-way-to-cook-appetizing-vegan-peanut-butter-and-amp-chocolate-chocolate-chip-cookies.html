Ingredients of Vegan Peanut Butter & Chocolate Chocolate Chip Cookies ^_^

  1. Prepare 1 cup of Whole Wheat Flour.
  2. Prepare 1 1/4 cup of Almond flour.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of Baking soda.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of Salt.
  5. It's 2 tbsp of Milled flax.
  6. You need 6 tbsp of Water.
  7. It's 3/4 cup of Pure Cane Sugar.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of Pure vanilla extract.
  9. Prepare 2/3 cup of Baking cocoa.
  10. You need 2/3 cup of Vegan peanut butter (crunchy-style).
  11. Prepare 1 cup of Vegan butter (I use Earth Balance).
  12. You need 3/4 cup of Brown sugar.
  13. It's 2 cup of Dairy-free/soy-free chocolate chips (I use Enjoy Life).

Vegan Peanut Butter & Chocolate Chocolate Chip Cookies ^_^ instructions

  1. Preheat oven 350°F.
  2. Make two flax eggs by thoroughly mixing the milled flax and water together. Set aside (one flax egg=1tbs milled flax and 3tbs of water)..
  3. Beat butter, peanut butter, sugars, vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Add flax eggs. Continue to beat until well combined..
  5. Thoroughly, combine flours, salt, baking soda and cocoa..
  6. Gradually, beat the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients..
  7. Stir in chocolate chips.
  8. Form small balls (I'll let you use your best judgment on that) from the dough and place on ungreased cookie sheet..
  9. Bake for 8 to 13 mins...depending on your oven.
