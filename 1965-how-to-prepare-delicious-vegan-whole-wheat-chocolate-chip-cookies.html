Ingredients of Vegan Whole Wheat Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. You need of Dry.
  2. You need 2 cup of whole wheat flour.
  3. It's 1/2 cup of vegan sugar.
  4. It's 3 tbsp of ground flax seed.
  5. Prepare 3 tsp of baking powder.
  6. It's 1 tsp of salt.
  7. It's of Wet.
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup of water.
  9. You need 1/3 cup of applesauce.
  10. It's 1/3 cup of vegetable oil.
  11. Prepare 1 tsp of apple cider vinegar.
  12. You need of Chocolate Chips.
  13. You need 1/2 of 10 oz. bag of vegan chocolate chips.

Vegan Whole Wheat Chocolate Chip Cookies step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F Fahrenheit.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Mix all wet ingredients together in another bowl.
  4. Mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop out tablespoons of dough and place on cookie sheet (these cookies are small, so I counted 2 cookies as one serving).
  7. Bake for 10 minutes.
