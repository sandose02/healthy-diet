Ingredients of Healthy Popsicle

  1. It's 2 tbsp of Quaker Oats.
  2. It's 1 of small banana.
  3. You need 1 tbsp of chia seeds.
  4. You need 1/2 cup of milk.
  5. You need 1-2 tbsp of honey.
  6. It's 3-4 of almonds.
  7. It's 2 tsp of Roohafza.

Healthy Popsicle step by step

  1. Take a grinder add oats and chia seeds, almonds grind well..
  2. Now add banana, honey, milk, Roohafza blend well for nicely combined..
  3. Pour into popsicle moulds..
  4. Refrigerate for 6-7 hours..
  5. Enjoy....
