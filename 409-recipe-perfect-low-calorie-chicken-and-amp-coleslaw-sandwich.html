Low Calorie Chicken & Coleslaw Sandwich instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the marinade ingredients together and add in the chicken breasts. Cover and keep in the fridge for at least 2 hours..
  2. Remove the chicken pieces from the fridge. Put them in a pan on medium heat and cook until they are well done..
  3. To prepare the coleslaw, mix the shredded cabbage leaves and carrots together in a bowl. Add in the light mayonnaise, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic and salt and mix well..
  4. Divide the chicken pieces among the 4 sandwich rolls. Place an adequate amount of coleslaw on top of the chicken pieces in each sandwich..
  5. Toast the sandwiches a little just so it changes color on the outside and eat hot..
