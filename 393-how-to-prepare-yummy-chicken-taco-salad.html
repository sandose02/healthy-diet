Chicken taco salad instructions

  1. cut totillas into 4 wedges each.
  2. preheat oven to 400°F.
  3. coat baking sheet with olive oil cooking spray.
  4. arrange tortillas on baking sheet, spray with cooking spray..
  5. bake until golden brown about 10 minutes.
  6. arrange tortillas in bottom and up sides of 4 salad bowls. Divide lettuce, chicken, tomatoes, and cheese amongst four bowls..
  7. Whisk together sour cream, salsa, cumin and pepper sauce. Drizzle about 3 tablespoons dressing over each salad.
