Low carb chocolate chip cookies step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Sift almond flour, coconut flour, baking soda, and salt into a mixing bowl..
  3. Mix remaining ingredients (excluding chocolate chips) with an electric mixer..
  4. Fold in chocolate chips with a spoon..
  5. Scoop chocolate chip cookie double battery with a melon baller or small scooper onto baking sheet that has been greased..
  6. Bake 8 to 12 minutes or until slightly brown..
  7. Enjoy!.
