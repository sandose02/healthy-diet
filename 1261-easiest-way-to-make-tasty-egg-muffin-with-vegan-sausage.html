Egg Muffin With Vegan Sausage instructions

  1. Add eggs, spinach and shredded cheese to blender.  Pulse blend for a very short time, just enough to beat the eggs and chop the spinach.  Set aside..
  2. In a skillet, cook chopped sausage in olive oil until crispy, about 10 minutes on medium heat..
  3. Add 3-4 pieces chopped sausage to the bottom of each muffin tray.  Fill 3/4 of the way with egg mixture..
  4. Bake at 350 for 20 min..
  5. Remove from oven and top with sliced white cheddar cheese..
