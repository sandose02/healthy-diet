Breakfast Pizza low carb instructions

  1. Preheat oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Crisp the bacon.
  2. Slice the hotdogs, olives and sausage.
  3. Mix the eggs and cheese, pour into a hot pan or skillet.
  4. Let the eggs get semi-firm put into oven for 3 minutes.
  5. Add cheddar cheese. Start at edge and work inwards.
  6. Add sliced hotdogs and sausage.
  7. Add bacon and olives.
  8. Add mozzarella and tomatoes.
  9. Put into oven for 20-25 minutes. Sprinkle with 2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese..
  10. Let rest for 12 minutes before serving.
