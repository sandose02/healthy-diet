Ingredients of Low carb raspberry gelatin desert

  3. You need 1 cup of uncooked pecans, chopped.
  4. Prepare 1/2 packages of sugar free cool whip.

Low carb raspberry gelatin desert step by step

  1. Open can of oranges and put in a strainer and set asside.
  2. Make jello according to package. (I use the small one because I'm only one person who eats on it.).
  3. In a sealable bowl, add jello mix and oranges and cool in fridge for 2 hours..
  4. Pull bowl out and break up jello mixture with a whisk. You will break up the oranges..
  5. Add pecans and cool whip and mix until everything is covered. And chill for one hour. Then serve it up..
