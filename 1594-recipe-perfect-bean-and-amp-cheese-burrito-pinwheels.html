Ingredients of Bean & Cheese Burrito Pinwheels

  1. You need 4 of whole wheat tortillas.
  2. It's 2 tbsp of freshed squeezed lemon juice.
  3. It's 1 of garlic clove, minced.
  4. It's 1 tsp of olive oil.
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp of salsa.
  6. It's 1 tbsp of fresh cilantro, chopped.
  7. You need 2 oz of low fat cheddar or Mexican blend cheese, shredded.
  8. It's 1 of Additional salsa and Greek yogurt to taste.
  9. Prepare 1 can of black beans (15oz), drained and rinsed.

Bean & Cheese Burrito Pinwheels instructions

  1. Warm tortillas according to package directions; set aside & keep warm.
  2. In a food processor, blend together black beans, lemon juice, garlic and olive oil.
  3. Put into a medium bowl and stir in salsa and cilantro.
  4. Evenly portion bean spread between tortillas. Roll up tightly and slice evenly into 1inch thick pieces..
  5. Serve with salsa and Greek yogurt, as desired.
