Hungry Girl's Healthy Cheesecake step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°. Spray a 9 inch cake pan with nonstick spray..
  2. In a large bowl combine cream cheese, stevia/truvia, && vanilla extract with an electric mixer set to medium speed. Beat until smooth/1-2 minutes..
  3. Continue to beat while gradually adding yogurt, egg white, lemon juice, flour, && cinnamon. Beat until smooth/about two minutes..
  4. Evenly pour mixture into the cake pan. Bake until firm/40-45 minutes. Let cool completely/about two hours..
  5. Chill in refrigerator for about one hour..
