Ingredients of Chicken & Mac Salad

  1. It's 1 can of chicken breast in water.
  2. Prepare 2 cup of mayonnaise.
  3. You need 1 cup of plain yogurt.
  4. It's 2 tbsp of sour cream.
  5. Prepare 1 box of wheat pasta noodles.
  6. It's 1 tbsp of spicy Brown mustard.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of pepper.
  8. It's 2 dash of hot sauce.
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp of garlic salt.
  10. It's 1 tsp of tajin fruit seasoning.
  11. You need 1/2 of diced purple onion.
  12. Prepare 1/2 of diced serrano chili.
