Ingredients of Vegetables Soup (Indonesian vegetarian soup =Sup Tahu)

  6. Prepare of Black/white pepper.

  8. You need of Chopped chinese celery for garnish.
  9. Prepare of Fried shallots for garnish.

Vegetables Soup (Indonesian vegetarian soup =Sup Tahu) step by step

  1. First, boil water in a saucepan. Add the chicken powder or mushrooms powder..
  2. Meanwhile pell the potato, carrots and celery cut in the shape as you desire..
  3. When the water is boiled then add the potatoes and carrot. Cook them until half cooked. Then add the tofu. Continue cook until the potatoes and carrot are soft but not over cooked or mushy..
  4. Blend the fried garlic and pepper. Add into the soup..
