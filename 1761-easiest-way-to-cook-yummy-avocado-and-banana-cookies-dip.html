Ingredients of Avocado and banana cookies dip

  5. You need 1 tsp of lemon juice.

Avocado and banana cookies dip instructions

  1. peel your banana.
  2. cut the banana into small pieces.
  3. put the pices in a blender.
  4. cut your avocado in half (remember that avocado has a hard seed inside which your knife wont cut).
  5. twist your avocado and open it.
  6. chop the knife carefully into the avocado seed and twist the seed with the knife and take it out.
  7. get the avocado out of its peel with a tablespoon.
  8. cut the avocado into pieces and add to banana.
  9. take the honey and add it to the fruit.
  10. add the lemon juice.
  11. cover your blender and blend untill the paste is all smooth.
  12. take your ready paste out and add the coconut (you can skip the coconut though if you don't like it).
  13. mix it all together with a spoon and your dip is ready!.
  14. TIP: you can use it as a face and hair moisturizing mask it really is great! but then don't add the coconut.
  15. TIP: you can use fake honey, it will only be less healthy.
  16. TIP: chceck if it's not to sweet for you, you can always add some more banana to reduce the sweetness or add more honey if it's not sweet enough.
