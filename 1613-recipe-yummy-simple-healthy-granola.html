Simple healthy granola.

You can cook Simple healthy granola using 11 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Simple healthy granola

  1. Prepare 150 g of old fashion oats.
  2. You need 30 g of pistachio.
  3. You need 30 g of walnuts.
  4. Prepare 60 ml of honey (maple syrup if vegan?).
  5. You need 1 tsp of salt.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of cinnamon.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of vanilla essence.
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp of coconut oil.
  9. It's 20 g of dessiccated coconut.
  10. It's 2 tbspn of black sesame.
  11. You need 50 g of dried mixed berries.

Simple healthy granola instructions

  1. Heat up coconut oil, honey and vanilla essence and in the bowl mix it with all of the ingredients apart of coconut and dried fruits.
  2. Spread it over on a roasting tray and bake in 150 °C for 20 min. Stir every few minutes.
  3. After 20 min add coconut and dried fruits and bake it further for 10 min.
  4. Cool it down.
  5. Serve it with milk/ yoghurt or alternatively soy/ almond milk. Add it to your puddings as another layer or texture..
