Vegan banana nut muffins- with crunchy oat topping instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°c,in a big bowl mash banana well or you can grind it in grinder..
  2. In mashed banana add vanilla essence, sugar, water, salt,mix well,.
  3. Sieve maida baking powder, baking soda, in banana mixture.add walnut mix well.
  4. Pour this mixture in mould.
  5. In small bowl add oats,oil, sugar,flour mix well,.
  6. Spoon on top of banana filling.
  7. Bake approximately 10-20 min,or until knife comes out clean..
