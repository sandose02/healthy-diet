Ingredients of Nearly vegan spinach pasta

  1. It's 1 of whole wheat penne pasta or red lentil penne pasta.
  2. Prepare 1 pack of vegan ground beef(beyond meat crumbled).
  3. It's 1/2 cup of almond milk.
  4. Prepare 1 oz of cream cheese.
  5. It's 2 tbsp of Parmesan cheese.
  6. Prepare 1 of garlic.
  7. You need 1 bag of spinach.
  8. Prepare 1 cup of chop bell pepper green or your fav color.
  9. You need 1 cup of chopped onion.
  10. You need 1 cup of chopped mushroom.
  11. Prepare 1 tsp of salt.
  12. You need 1 tsp of pepper.
  13. You need 1 tsp of oregano.
  14. It's 1 tsp of dried parsley.
  15. It's 2 tbsp of Virgin Olive oil.

Nearly vegan spinach pasta instructions

  1. For spinach sauce: in a blender or food processor add spinach, cream cheese, garlic, almond milk,Parmesan cheese and blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Cook pasta as per box instructions.
  3. In a sauce pan add olive oil, bell peppers, onion,mushroom until tender then add ground beef and cook on low heat for 3 mins. Stir occasionally.
  4. Add handful of spinach, pasta to the sauce pan then spinach sauce. If sauce too thick add a lil more almond milk as desired. Season with salt pepper, oregano, parsley. Stir and cover n let simmer for 5mins and low heat. Serve and enjoy.
