Ingredients of vegan tofu scramble

  1. You need 14 oz of firm tofu, drained and pressed dry.
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp of vegan butter.
  3. It's 1/2 tsp of turmeric.
  4. You need 2 tbsp of salt free spice blend (like a ms. dash).
  5. It's 1 cup of frozen/thawed chopped spinach.
  6. It's 1 of handful of vegan cheese (optional).
  7. Prepare 2 tsp of maple syrup (optional).
  8. You need 1/2 cup of chopped onion (optional).

vegan tofu scramble instructions

  1. heat up non-stick skillet and add butter..
  2. put in tofu and use metal spatula to break up into bits as big or as small as you want..
  3. add in turmeric and spices..
  4. keep chopping and flipping tofu..
  5. add spinach. cook until tofu is cooked through..
  6. add optional ingredients and serve hot.
  7. you can try it in a burrito too.
