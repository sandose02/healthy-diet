Chinese Chicken Salad step by step

  1. Cook chicken breasts any way desired..
  2. Toast the almonds and sesame seeds if necessary..
  3. Chop the cabbage finely, and crumble the ramen noodles to resemble little croutons..
  4. For the dressing, mix all the ingredients together. Pour on top of salad and mix..
  5. Allow to sit for 15 minutes so the ramen chunks will absorb the dressing..
  6. Serves 6-8.
