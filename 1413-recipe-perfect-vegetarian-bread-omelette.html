Ingredients of Vegetarian Bread Omelette

  1. You need 1/2 cup of besan.
  2. Prepare 1 of onion.
  3. It's 1 of tomato.
  4. Prepare 1 of green chilli.
  5. It's 1 of small bunch coriander.
  6. You need as per taste of Salt, turmeric, chilli powder.
  7. You need 1 tsp of Coriander cumin powder.
  8. Prepare 2 of bread slices.
  9. It's 1 tbsp of Oil.

Vegetarian Bread Omelette step by step

  1. Add all dry spices to besan..
  2. Chop onion, tomato, coriander and chilli in chopper..
  3. Add this to besan. Mix thoroughly and make batter..
  4. Pour and spread batter in non stick tava. Put a bread on it..
  5. Cover and let it cook..
  6. Turn and Cook it on other side..
  7. Vegetarian bread omelette is ready to serve. Enjoy with ketchup...
