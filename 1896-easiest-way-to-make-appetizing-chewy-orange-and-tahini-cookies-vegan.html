Ingredients of Chewy orange and tahini cookies - vegan

  1. You need 2 cups of ground almonds.
  2. It's 1 tsp of ground cinnamon.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of ground cardamom.
  4. It's 1/2 tsp of flaky sea salt.
  5. You need 3/4 cup of tahini.
  6. It's 1/2 cup of maple syrup.
  7. It's 2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  8. Prepare of Zest of one orange.
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of orange blossom water (not essential but good!).
  10. Prepare of Almonds (blanched - whole or flakes) + sea salt to garnish.
