Ingredients of Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. It's 1 1/4 Cup of Flour.
  2. It's 1/2 tsp of Baking Soda.
  3. It's 1/2 tsp of Salt.
  4. It's 1 tsp of Cornflour.
  5. It's 1/2 Cup of Vegan Margarine.
  6. You need 1/2 Cup of Brown Sugar.
  7. It's 1/4 Cup of Sugar.
  8. It's 1 tbsp of Milk (of your choice).
  9. You need 1 tsp of Vanilla.
  10. You need 3/4 Cup of Vegan Mini Chocolate Chips.

Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and line baking tray with parchment paper..
  2. In a bowl, whisk the flour, baking soda, salt and cornflour..
  3. In a separate bowl, add the margarine, sugars, milk and vanilla. Beat until combined..
  4. Add half of the flour mixture into the wet mixture and gently combine. Add the rest of the flour mixture and mix until just combined..
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips..
  6. For each cookie, scoop 1tsp of dough and roll it into a ball. Place the rounded scoops into your baking sheet with enough room for them to spread..
  7. Bake for 7-9 minutes until done to your liking..
