Ingredients of Two Minute Scramble

  1. Prepare 2 medium of eggs.
  2. You need 2 tbsp of fat-free milk.
  3. It's 2 tbsp of low fat cheese,shredded.
  4. It's 1 of salt & pepper to taste.

Two Minute Scramble instructions

  1. Coat a 12-ounce microwave safe coffee mug or bowl with cooking spray..
  2. Add eggs & milk;beat until blended..
  3. Microwave on high for 45 seconds;stir. Continue to microwave until eggs are almost set, for 30 or 45 seconds longer..
  4. Top with cheese; season with salt & pepper..
  5. Serve directly from cup, or scoop onto a toasted English muffin or whole-wheat toast & serve..
