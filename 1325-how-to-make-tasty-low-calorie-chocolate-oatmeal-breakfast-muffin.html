Ingredients of low calorie chocolate oatmeal breakfast muffin

  1. Prepare 1 3/4 cup of rolled oats.
  2. You need 3 of egg whites.
  3. You need 3/4 cup of unsweetened cocoa.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of unsweetened applesauce.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of caramel extract.
  6. It's 1/2 cup of plain low fat yogurt.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of vinegar.
  8. It's 1 1/2 tsp of baking powder.
  9. You need 1 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  10. You need 1/4 tsp of salt.
  11. It's 1 cup of Hot water.
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup of sweetener that measures like sugar.
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup of chocolate chips.

low calorie chocolate oatmeal breakfast muffin step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix all Ingredients except the chocolate chips.
  3. Once combined, stir in half of chocolate chips.
  4. Spoon into muffin pan coated with nonstick cooking spray.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven and sprinkle with remaining chocolate chips.
  7. Bake for 10 more minutes or until toothpick inserted comes out clean.
