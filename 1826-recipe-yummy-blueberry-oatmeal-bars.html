Blueberry Oatmeal Bars instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line 8x8 inch pan with parchment paper & spray with cooking spray.
  2. Base & crumble topping: In large bowl, mix together oat flour, oats, brown sugar, cinnamon, baking soda, & salt. Add in melted butter, vanilla & almond extract. Stir until a nice crumble-like dough forms..
  3. Place 1 3/4 cups of the mixture into pan & press down evenly with hands. put remaining crumble in fridge for later..
  4. Blueberry filling: place a pot over medium heat. add blueberries, syrup, almond extract, cornstarch, and salt. Use wooden spoon to press down on blueberries a bit..
  5. Bring pot to a boil, then reduce heat and cook for 5-10 more minutes or until mixture is thicccccc.
  6. Pour blueberry filling into pan over the crust and spread evenly..
  7. Use the remaining crumble from the fridge to sprinkle over the filling.
  8. Bake for 30 minutes or until blueberry filling is bubbly & crumble is golden.
  9. Allow bars to cool completely before cutting. serve immediately plain or with ice cream for a sweet treat (:.
