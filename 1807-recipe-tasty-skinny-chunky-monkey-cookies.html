Ingredients of skinny chunky monkey cookies

  1. You need 3 of ripe bananas.
  2. Prepare 2 cup of old fashion quick oats.
  3. It's 1/4 cup of creamy peanut butter.
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of cocoa or cacao powder.
  5. You need 1/3 cup of unsweetened apple sauce.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of vanilla extract.

skinny chunky monkey cookies instructions

  1. preheat overn to 350.
  2. mash ripe bananas in large bowl.
  3. let set for approx. 20 minutes.
  4. stir in remaining ingredients.
  5. let batter stand approx 20 minutes.
  6. drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet.
  7. bake 10-12 minutes.
