Pumpkin ice cream pie instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 and allow Halo Top to start melting.
  2. Crumble cereal bars and place in a mixer.
  3. Add in power cake mix.
  4. 2 tbsp of low fat cream cheese blend till crumbs are even and uniform.
  5. Place in pie shell and spread out evenly. Spray the exposed sides of dish with baking spray.
  6. Make sure Halo Top is soft and partially melted.
  7. Combine ice cream and pumpkin pie filling.
  8. Add in eggs.
  9. Combine till uniform, scrape down sides of bowl.
  10. Place over crumb mixture.
  11. Bake at 425 for 15 then reduce temp to 350 bake for an additional 30-40.
  12. I checked my pie at 30 and it was done. Insert a knife in center and make sure it comes out clean. Allow to cool before serving.
