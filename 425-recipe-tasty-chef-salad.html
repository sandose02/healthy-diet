Chef Salad step by step

  1. Chop and rinse iceberg lettuce..
  2. Prep turkey bacon in microwave for 1:20sec.
  3. Add 2 cups of lettuce to bowl. Topping with 3oz of cubed chicken breast.
  4. Diced 1 hard boiled egg, and spread on top of salad..
  5. Chop cooked turkey bacon, causing it to crumble into homemade bits, sprinkle over top of salad..
  6. Measure a 1/4 of your cheese and sprinkle of top.
  7. Enjoy!.
