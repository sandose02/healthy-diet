No-cheese cheesecake! step by step

  1. Roast oats in a hot pan, or in the oven until golden..
  2. Pour in a tray, mix with egg whites, honey, and cinnamon until having a good consistency..
  3. Blend the milk, yogurt, surgery, and vanilla until smooth..
  4. Pour in the tray over the oat crust..
  5. Heat in medium temperature for 20-30 minutes (until consistent)..
  6. Cool down in the fridge..
  7. Cut into medium squares, add the topping, and enjoy! ?.
