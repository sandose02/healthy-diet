Weight Watcher's Southwest Easy-Oven Chicken step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. In 9x13 inch baking pan, combine broth, beans, salsa and garlic..
  3. Add chicken and baste with liquid.
  4. Bake, stirring once, until chicken is cooked through and liquid is reduced. About 45 minutes.
  5. WW points= 4. 241 calories.
