Healthy Eggs Benedict.

You can cook Healthy Eggs Benedict using 7 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Healthy Eggs Benedict

  1. It's 1 of whole wheat english muffin.
  2. You need 2 slice of weight watchers american cheese.
  3. Prepare 2 slice of lite ham.
  4. You need 2 of eggs.
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of white vinegar.
  6. Prepare 4 cup of water.
  7. It's 1 of salt & pepper for taste.

Healthy Eggs Benedict step by step

  1. put water in small pot & bring to a boil.
  2. cut whole wheat english muffin in half & toast in toaster....when done put one slice of cheese on each half & close toater oven door & with toaster off let cheese melt on its own while toaster is off.
  3. place 2 slices of ham on dish & heat in microwave on high for 15 seconds.
  4. when water starts boiling put in vinigar & gently crack eggs close to boiling water & gently drop in water.
  5. take out english muffin & plate & put a slice of ham on each half.
  6. poach eggs until desired doneness...about 3 minutes depending on size of eggs...remove with slotted spoon to drain any water and place one egg on each half & serve.
  7. salt & pepper for taste.
