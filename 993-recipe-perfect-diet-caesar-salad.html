Ingredients of Diet Caesar Salad

  1. You need 1 of head romaine lettuce, cut into bite-size pieces.
  2. You need 3/4 cup of croutons.
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese, grated.
  4. You need of Dressing:.
  5. It's 4 tablespoons of olive oil.
  6. Prepare 3 tablespoons of lemon juice.
  7. It's 2 tablespoons of red vinegar.
  8. You need 1 teaspoon of dijon mustard.
  9. You need 1 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce.
  10. It's 1 clove of garlic, crushed.
  11. You need 3 of flat anchovies, minced (optional).

Diet Caesar Salad step by step

  1. In a bowl, mix the lemon juice, vinegar, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, anchovies if using, salt and pepper until well-combined..
  2. Slowly add in the olive oil in a steady flow while constantly whisking until the dressing is smooth..
  3. In a salad bowl, add in the lettuce and half the dressing and toss. Then add the remaining dressing along with the parmesan cheese, and croutons and toss again..
