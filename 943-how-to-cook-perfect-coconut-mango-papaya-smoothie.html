Ingredients of Coconut Mango Papaya Smoothie

  1. Prepare 1 of small ripe papaya.
  2. You need 1 of ripe mango.
  3. You need 1/4 cup of cold low fat milk/almond milk.
  4. You need 6 tbsp of coconut milk.
  5. It's of honey or any sweetener as desired.
  6. You need 1 teaspoon of chia seeds to garnish.
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of desiccated coconut/coconut flakes to garnish.

Coconut Mango Papaya Smoothie step by step

  1. Peel, de-seed and chop papaya into cubes. Similarly peel and chop mangoes into cubes. Freeze the fruits.
  2. Blend all the ingredients to form smooth drink. Adjust the quantity of milk as both papaya and mangoes thicken on blending. Pour in tall cold serving glass.
  3. Garnish with chia seeds, desiccated coconut. Serve cold.
