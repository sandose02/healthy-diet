Ingredients of Pasta salad

  1. You need of Dressing:.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of olive oil.
  3. It's 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar.
  4. Prepare 1/4 of mayo.
  5. You need 2 Tbs of spicy mustard.
  6. Prepare 2 Tbs of sugar.
  7. Prepare to taste of S&P.
  8. Prepare of Salad:.
  9. It's 2 cups of cooked macaroni.

  11. It's 1 package of matchstick carrots.

Pasta salad step by step

  1. Cook macaroni according to package..
  2. While pasta cooks, add all dressing ingredients and whisk together.
  3. In the last 5 minutes of cooking, add broccoli and carrots to boiling water..
  4. Cook until pasta is al dente.
  5. Rinse pasta and veggies until very cool. Add to dressing..
  6. Add all other ingredients and mix thoroughly..
  7. Refrigerate over night..
