Beach Ball Party Tray instructions

  1. On a round serving tray, place a round serving bowl or container of veggie dip at the top.
  2. Make an outline with veggie dip going from the bottom of the serving bowl to the bottom.
  3. Place each veggie of your choice in coordinating colors of a beach ball, filling between each veggie dip line.
  4. For the Octopus or crab garnishment you will want to cut the bottom off a bell pepper and clean the seeds out.
  5. Take two black olives and dip them in the veggie dip on one side, then stick them onto the bell pepper towards the rounded top to make eyes..
  6. The black olives will not sit well, so place a toothpick through each eye to balance it and hold it in place..
  7. Then take another bell pepper and cut quarter inch length wise slices to make legs..
  8. Cut a slit on each side of the bell pepper and position it on back of serving bowl.
  9. place legs accordingly.
