Ingredients of Mom's Breakfast Bars

Mom's Breakfast Bars instructions

  1. Heat peanut butter, maple syrup, and rice syrup in a sauce pan over medium low heat.
  2. Stir until smooth and melted.
  3. Add cinnamon, salt, vanilla, and oats.
  4. Stir until well mixed.
  5. Pour into and 8x8 pan lined with parchment paper and bake at 350 for 18 minutes.
  6. Remove from pan, set on cooling rack, and cut.
