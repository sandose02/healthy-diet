Healthy oatmeal raisin cookie step by step

  1. Preheat oven for 350 degrees F..
  2. In a large bowl, combine sugar, oil, egg, and vanilla.
  3. Mix all dry ingredients.
  4. Combine both dry and wet ingredients.
  5. Use 1-tablespoon-sized spoon scoop dough on baking sheet.
  6. Sprinkle some sea salt on each dough before baking.
  7. Bake for 16 minutes.
