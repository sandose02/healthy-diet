Fruit Salad with Palm Sugar Dressing *Vegan step by step

  1. Cut up all the fruits, place them in a platter.
  2. Fry peanuts, mash them in a mashing plate/bowl, add some chilies if you like, add palm sugar, mash them until they mixed well..
  3. Mix tamarind with water, add 1 tbs of tamarind water into palm sugar mixture, mix well..
  4. Enjoy it with any fruits you like.
