HCG diet meal 7 & 9: fish fillet with string beans instructions

  1. Defrost and squeeze out the string beans and weigh them to 200 g or a bit less if you will add other veggies..
  2. Weigh fish fillets to 100 g.
  3. Chop tomato in half and remove pulp. then cut into bite size bits..
  4. Place all ingredients in a pan or tin foil add spices and 1/2 cup of water. place in the oven at 180 c and bake for 35 minutes or till the fish and beans are cooked. cover with foil.
