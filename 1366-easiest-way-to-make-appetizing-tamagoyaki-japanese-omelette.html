Tamagoyaki (Japanese omelette) instructions

  1. Heat a small frying pan very hot over the hob.
  2. Add two eggs, pop yolks, allow to partially cook..
  3. Add soy sauce.
  4. Sprinkle on half of the sugar (to taste).
  5. Fold over so they cover only half the pan.
  6. Do same to the other two eggs on the remaining side of the pan.
  7. Add more soy sauce.
  8. Sprinkle on the other half of the sugar (to taste).
  9. when partially cooked fold over onto other eggs.
  10. Fold the whole lot so it looks a little like a folded crepe (pancake).
  11. Turn it all over.
  12. Allow eggs to fully cook then remove onto a plate.
  13. Sprinkle some soy sauce and sugar on top (to taste).
  14. Cut into bitesize pieces.
