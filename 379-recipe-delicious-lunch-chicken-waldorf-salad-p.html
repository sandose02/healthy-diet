Ingredients of LUNCH - CHICKEN WALDORF SALAD (P)

  1. It's 6 oz of Cooked chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes.
  2. Prepare 1/2 medium of Granny Smith apple, thinly sliced.
  3. It's 2 tbsp of chopped raw walnuts.
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of halved red grapes.
  5. It's 2 tbsp of citrus Vinaigrette.
