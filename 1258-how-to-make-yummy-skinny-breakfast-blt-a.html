Ingredients of Skinny Breakfast B.L.T. + A.

  1. It's 2 of slices of healthy bread of choice (I used WW wheat,only 2 pts for 2 slices).
  2. You need 4 tbsp of egg whites or egg beaters.
  3. You need 1 of slice of low fat cheese(I used Sargento ultra thin).
  4. Prepare 1 of baby spinach leaves(or lettuce of choice).
  5. You need 1 of thick slice of tomato.
  6. Prepare 1/4 of of an avocado, thinly sliced.
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of light mayonaisse.
  8. It's 1 of slice of turkey bacon, torn in half.
  9. It's 1 of freshly cracked pepper.

Skinny Breakfast B.L.T. + A. step by step

  1. Toss bread in the toaster. While the bread is toasting spray a pan and cook the egg whites. When cooked,add cheese slice on top to melt slightly..
  2. On 1 side of toast spread 1/2 tbsp of light mayo then layer spinach, tomato, cooked egg white with cheese, turkey bacon and finally avocado slices. Pepper generously! Spread remaining 1/2 tbsp of light mayo on other toast slice. Place it on top and dive in!!.
