Ingredients of Vegan Oreo/Chocolate Cookies

  1. It's 185 g of Plain Flour.
  2. It's Half of Tsp Baking Powder.
  3. Prepare 60 ml of Coconut Milk.
  4. You need 50 g of White Sugar.
  5. Prepare 100 g of Brown Sugar.
  6. Prepare 100 g of Coconut Oil.
  7. You need 1 Tsp of Vanilla Extract.
  8. Prepare 50 g of Dark Chocolate.
  9. Prepare 50 g of Oreo Cookies.

Vegan Oreo/Chocolate Cookies instructions

  1. Combine White and Brown Sugar and Coconut oil in a large bowl and mix. Whisk in the Coconut Milk and Vanilla Extract until the sugar starts to dissolve. Sift in the flour, followed by Baking powder and fold the mixture gently..
  2. Separate half the mixture into another bowl and in each batter, add crushed Oreo cookies and the other chopped Dark Chocolate/Dark Choc Chip. Place both bowls in the fridge to chill for 30minutes while pre-heating the oven at 180C..
  3. Using a teaspoon, scoop a spoonful of dough and form into a ball before adding onto a large flat baking tray. Make sure each dough ball is social distancing from one another at 2 inches and bake for 12-15minutes. Once theyâ€™re baked, leave the cookies to cool for further 15minutes..
