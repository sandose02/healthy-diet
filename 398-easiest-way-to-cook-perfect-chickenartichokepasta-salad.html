chicken...artichoke...pasta salad step by step

  1. 1 7 oz jar roasted red peppers, drained & cut into strips..
  2. drain the artichokes, reserving marinade. set aside..
  3. coat large skillet w/olive oil. preheat over Med. heat..
  4. add chicken & garlic to skillet & cook til chicken is brown all over. add the artichoke marinade., the 1/4 c chick broth & the wine..
  5. bring to a boil & reduce heat..
  6. cover and simmer for ten min. stir in the artichokes, red peppers & olives and fresh oregano. heat thru.
  7. spoon chicken over pasta and sprinkle with feta cheese.
