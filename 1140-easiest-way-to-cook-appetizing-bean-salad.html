Ingredients of Bean Salad

  1. You need 500 g of green beans.
  2. Prepare 19 oz of can black beans drained.
  3. You need 2 of chopped seeded tomatoes.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of canned or cooked kernel corn.
  5. Prepare 3 of sliced green onions.
  6. It's 2 Tbsp of fresh lime juice.
  7. Prepare 2 Tbsp of olive oil.
  8. It's 1 of crushed garlic clove.
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp of salt.
  10. You need 1/4 tsp of cayenne.
  11. Prepare of Chopped fresh cilantro.

Bean Salad instructions

  1. Boil green beans till crisp tender then let cool..
  2. Mix beans with all ingredients up to and including green onions..
  3. Whisk together lime juice, olive oil, garlic, salt and cayenne..
  4. Toss bean salad with dressing. Top with cilantro..
  5. Enjoy!.
