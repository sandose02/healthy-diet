Chicken or Tofu Lettuce Wraps instructions

  1. Cook chicken strips for 5 to 7 mins or until slightly brown.
  2. While chicken is cooking, begin chopping desired vegetables such as carrots and mushrooms.
  3. Once chicken is cooked, add vegetables to pan of chicken. Continue to cook..
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of Soy Sauce to chicken..
  5. Add 1 tablespoon of sugar to pan..
  6. Add 1 tablespoon of Rice Vinegar to chicken..
  7. Stir all ingredients together for about 2 mins..
  8. Peel lettuce head for each leaf..
  9. Add to scoops of stir fry to one lettuce leaf and Enjoy..
  10. Substituting the chicken or tofu is a great option for my fellow Vegan or Vegetarians..
