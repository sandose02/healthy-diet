Ingredients of Campfire Taco Salad

  1. Prepare 6 of snack size bags (1 1/2 ounces each) corn chips.
  2. Prepare 1 can of (15 ounces) chili without beans.
  3. It's 3/4 cup of sour cream.
  4. It's 1 jar of (8 ounces) mild salsa.
  5. Prepare 3 cup of (12 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese.
  6. You need 1/2 medium of head iceberg lettuce, shredded.

Campfire Taco Salad instructions

  1. Cut the top off each bag of chips..
  2. Set aside..
  3. Place chili in a saucepan..
  4. Cook on a grill over medium heat for 10 minutes or until heated through..
  5. Stir occasionally..
  6. Spoon about 2 tablespoons of chili into each bag of chips..
  7. Top with cheese, sour cream, salsa and lettuce..
  8. *You can also add tomatoes, onions, or other toppings if desired.*.
