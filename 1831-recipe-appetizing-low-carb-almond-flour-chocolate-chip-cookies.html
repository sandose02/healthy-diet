Ingredients of Low carb Almond flour Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. You need 2 tbsp of solid coconut oil.
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp of real maple syrup.
  3. It's 1 of large egg.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  5. You need 2 c of almond flour or almond meal.
  6. It's 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp of fine grain sea salt.
  8. Prepare 1/2 c of mini semi-sweet chocolate chips.

Low carb Almond flour Chocolate Chip Cookies step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F..
  2. Mix coconut oil (soften slightly if necessary) with maple syrup. Add egg and vanilla extract..
  3. Mix together the almond flour, baking soda and sea salt. Gradually add the wet ingredients. Mix in chocolate chips..
  4. Drop by spoonfuls onto cookie sheets. Bake for 8-9 min. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before eating..
