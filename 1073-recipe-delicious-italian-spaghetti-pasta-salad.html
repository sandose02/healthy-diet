Italian spaghetti pasta salad step by step

  1. Cook box of spaghetti, drain, Cool off pasta by running cold water over it when still in colander. put cooked spaghetti in a big bowl,chill in refrigerator for 15-20 mins..
  2. Add the cut veggies to the chilled cooked spaghetti and mix well..
  3. Make dressing or can skip this step and use Italian dressing. Add all dressing ingredients to a bowl, add wet ingredients and mix well..
  4. Add dressing to spaghetti mixture, and the pepperoni mix well.
  5. Chill in the fridge for four hours or over night. (this is good just chilled at 2 hrs).
