Ingredients of thermomix, low fat carbonara

  1. Prepare 1 of zucchini.
  2. It's 20 grams of olive oil.
  3. Prepare 50 grams of white wine.
  4. Prepare 550 grams of water.
  5. It's 375 grams of evaporated lite milk.
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of vege stock.
  7. It's 300 grams of spaghetti.
  8. Prepare 175 grams of bacon.
  9. It's 1 of egg.
  10. It's 50 grams of parmesan.

thermomix, low fat carbonara instructions

  1. grate parmesan 8 speed 10 sec, remove and set aside.
  2. chop zucchini speed 5, 3-4 secs.
  3. add olive oil, cook 100°, speed 1 reverse, 3 mins.
  4. add wine, water, milk, vege stock, cook 10 mins, 100°, speed 2 reverse.
  5. add spaghetti broken into thirds and bacon cut into thin strips, cook 100° 10 mins speed 2 reverse.
  6. whisk 1 egg and pour into running machine, reverse, speed 4 for a few seconds.
  7. pour into thermoserver and let sit a few mins.
  8. serve with parmesan.
