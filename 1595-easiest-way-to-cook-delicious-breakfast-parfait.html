Ingredients of Breakfast parfait

  1. It's 1 cup of low fat Vanilla yogurt.
  2. Prepare 6 of Blackberries.
  3. It's 5 of Raspberries.
  4. Prepare 2 large of Strawberry- sliced.
  5. It's 1/3 of Banana- sliced.
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp of Ground granola.
  7. You need 1 tsp of Raisins.

Breakfast parfait instructions

  1. Fill one quarter of a clear plastic parfait cup like the ones at mcdonalds.
  2. Add the fruit in whichever order you like, then add another quarter of the yogurt over that and add more fruit.
  3. Repeat so there are 4 layers of fruit (or 3 if you want more yogurt) with layers of yogurt on top of each layer of fruit.
  4. Add granola and raisins for topping. Enjoy!.
  5. ***Note*** this recipe can be altered to preference. You may want more or less yogurt and more or less fruit. Also, can substitute some fruits for others, or remove some, for example, if you don't like raisins.
