Mom's Tomato Melts step by step

  1. Core tomato and slice into 1/4 inch slices..
  2. Split English muffins so you have 4 halves. Toast to desired doneness..
  3. Remove toasted muffins from the toaster and place on oven safe tray..
  4. Put a slice of tomato on each toasted muffin half..
  5. Season tomatoes with your favorite spices or leave them plain like I do..
  6. Add a slice of cheese on top of each tomato slice..
  7. Put in oven and broil or bake until cheese is melted and browned to your liking..
  8. Enjoy!! This was one of my favorite ways to enjoy fresh tomatoes from the garden..
