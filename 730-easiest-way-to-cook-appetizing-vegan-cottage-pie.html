Ingredients of Vegan cottage pie

  1. Prepare 1 of onion.
  2. It's 1 of carrot.
  3. It's 1 cup of vegetable gravy.
  4. Prepare 5 of potatoes.
  5. It's 1 1/4 cup of soya milk.
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of margarine.
  7. You need 1 1/2 cup of frozen peas.
  8. You need 1 cup of soya mince.

Vegan cottage pie step by step

  1. preheat oven 200C.
  2. chop finely and fry onions and carrot.
  3. add peas, soya mince and gravy.
  4. peel, chop and boil potatoes until soft.
  5. mash potatoes with soya milk and margarine.
  6. put the mince mix on a deep oven tray and lay mash potatoes on top.
  7. leave in the oven until golden.
