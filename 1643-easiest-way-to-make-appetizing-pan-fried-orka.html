Ingredients of Pan Fried Orka

  1. Prepare 200 grams of orka, stems removed and sliced in half.
  2. It's of a good pinch of salt.
  3. You need 1-2 tbsp of olive oil.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of fresh lemon juice.

Pan Fried Orka instructions

  1. Rinse the orka under running water and then dry with paper towels. Prepare your orka..
  2. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan..
  3. Once its hot enough add the orka and sprinkle salt and the lemon juice..
  4. Let it fry and become slightly charred. Shake the pan regularly so the orka doesn't stick..
  5. Cook between 5-10 minutes. Add a splash more of olive oil if needs be..
  6. Remove from the pan and enjoy..
