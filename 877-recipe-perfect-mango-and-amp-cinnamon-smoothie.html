  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon of Cinnamon.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of Ice Cubes.
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup of Yogurt or Low-Fat Yogurt.
  6. You need 1 tablespoon of Honey.
