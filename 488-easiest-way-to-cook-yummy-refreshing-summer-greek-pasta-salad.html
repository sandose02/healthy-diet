Refreshing Summer Greek Pasta Salad.

You can cook Refreshing Summer Greek Pasta Salad using 17 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Refreshing Summer Greek Pasta Salad

  1. Prepare 2 cups of your favorite pasta (uncooked).
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 cups of cherry or plum tomatoes (halved).
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cups of cucumber (peeled and cubed).
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of red onion (sliced and cut about an inch long).
  5. It's 1/8 cup of feta cheese crumbles.
  6. You need 1/8 cup of Greek olives (sliced).
  7. It's 1/16 cup of shaved Parmesan.
  8. Prepare of Dressing:.
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup of Olive Oil (or other neutral oil).
  10. You need 4 tbs of red wine vinegar.
  11. It's 1 tbs of fresh squeezed lemon juice.
  12. It's 2 tsp of garlic (minced).
  13. Prepare 2 tsp of dried oregano.
  14. You need 1 tsp of honey.
  15. It's 1/2 tsp of kosher salt.
  16. You need 1/2 tsp of fresh cracked black pepper.
  17. Prepare 1/2 tsp of crushed red pepper.

Refreshing Summer Greek Pasta Salad step by step

  1. Cook pasta to package directions. Drain and wash with cold water and let cool..
  2. Combine all dressing ingredients and whisk to fully mix..
  3. Add all chopped veggies, cheeses and dressing to cooled pasta and toss. Refrigerate for at least an hour before serving. ENJOY!!.
