Ingredients of Creamy Mushrooms On Toast - Vegan Friendly.

  1. It's 4 slice of of whole meal seeded bread.
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp of Avacado oil (or any other oil, of course).
  3. You need 1 tbsp of Freshly squeezed lime juice.
  4. It's 1 small of onion- diced.
  5. It's 8 of Sliced mushrooms (I prefer chestnut mushrooms).
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of Soy sauce.
  7. You need 2 tbsp of Soy cream (you can use vegan yoghurt if preferred).

Creamy Mushrooms On Toast - Vegan Friendly. instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180c/350f..
  2. Bake bread for 5mins..
  3. Meanwhile, fry the onion and mushrooms in the oil and lime juice until soft. Add cream and soy sauce..
  4. Cut toast to triangles and arrange on 2 plates..
  5. Pour over mushroom mix and garnish with some parsley..
