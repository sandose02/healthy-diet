Ingredients of Basic Vegan Tofu Scramble

  1. It's 1 block of tofu (water pressed out).
  2. You need 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  3. You need 2 tsp of soy sauce.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of onion powder.
  5. It's 1 tsp of garlic powder.
  6. You need 2 tsp of oregano.
  7. You need 2 tsp of pepper.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of turmeric, optional, if egg-like appearance is desired.
  9. You need 1/2 cup of vegan cheddar cheese.

Basic Vegan Tofu Scramble step by step

  1. Heat pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil to hot pan.
  3. Add crumbled tofu to pan before pan gets hot.
  4. Season tofu with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and oregano..
  5. Stir constantly until tofu begins to crisp, about 10 minutes after tofu begins to sizzle..
  6. Turn off heat. Add soy sauce last minute to avoid over caramelization..
  7. Top with Vegan Cheddar Cheese and cover in pan, over hot burner, for 5-10 minutes until melted..
  8. Enjoy!.
