Ingredients of Mini Wagon Wheel Pasta Salad

  1. Prepare 1 (16 ounce) of package mini wagon wheel pasta (or any other pasta shape you have).
  2. You need 1 cup of shredded carrots (raw).
  3. It's 2/4 cups of sweet pickle relish (plus just a little extra).
  4. Prepare 3 teaspoons of onion salt.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of mayonnaise.
  6. It's 1 cup of sour cream.
  7. It's 2/4 cups of milk.
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper.
  9. You need 1-2 teaspoons of white vinegar.
