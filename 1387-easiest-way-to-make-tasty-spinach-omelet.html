Spinach Omelet instructions

  1. Melt 25 g of the butter in a pan. Add the spinach and cook over a gently heat for 4 to 5 minutes,stirring occasionally. Remove from the heat and stir in the yoghurt. Season with nutmeg,salt and pepper to taste and keep hot..
  2. Beat the eggs in a bowl,with salt and pepper to taste. Melt the remaining fat in a 28cm omelet pan. When the butter the butter is foaming, pour in the eggs. When the omelet begins to set, lift the cooked edge to allow the unset egg to run onto the pan..
  3. Place the fill in the center of the omelet, cook for one minute then fold in half. Slide onto a warmed plate and serve immediately..
