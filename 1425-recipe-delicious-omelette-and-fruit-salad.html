Omelette and fruit salad step by step

  1. Lay everything out.
  2. Beat the eggs and put them in a warm pan.
  3. Add spinach and let it steam down for a couple of minutes.
  4. Prepare avocado for chopping.
  5. Roughly chop peppers, cheese, avocado, apple. Drizzle with honey, season with salt and pepper, then hand toss..
  6. Steam spinach till fully limp.
  7. Fold omelette over.
  8. The finished article. It tasted better than it looks.
