Buffalo Wing Dip instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Remove excess fat from semi-frozen chicken, then dice chicken into bit sized pieces. Spray saute pan with cooking spray and cook chicken pieces until throughly cooked..
  2. Place chicken in a large mixing bowl and pour over Frank's hot sauce. Cover and set aside..
  3. Spread cream cheese into a 9x12 pan. Evenly layer chicken pieces. Then spread bleu cheese on top of chicken. Next, sprinkle shredded cheese on top of mixture..
  4. Place in oven and bake until cheese is melted and mixture is hot (about 20 minutes)..
  5. Remove and serve with taco chips of your choice..
