Low-Carb Gratin with Okara.

You can cook Low-Carb Gratin with Okara using 10 ingredients and 9 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Low-Carb Gratin with Okara

  1. It's of [For the white sauce]:.
  2. It's 2 tbsp of Okara powder.
  3. It's 200 ml of Milk (or soy milk).
  4. You need 30 grams of Butter.
  5. It's 2 slice of Processed cheese (melting type).
  6. It's 15 ml of Heavy cream (optional).
  7. Prepare 1 of Salt and pepper.
  8. Prepare 1 of Soup stock granules.
  9. You need of Cheese (to add on top).
  10. You need 1 of Mayonnaise.

Low-Carb Gratin with Okara step by step

  1. Coat a frying pan with mayonnaise, fry the ingredients of your choice, and set them aside on a plate..
  2. Melt the butter in the frying pan from Step 1. Make sure not to let it burn..
  3. Once the butter has melted, gradually add the okara powder little by little, mixing well to prevent lumps from forming. Be careful not to let it burn..
  4. When the okara powder becomes smooth, gradually add the milk (or soy milk) little by little, mixing well to make it even smoother..
  5. Break up the cheese and add to the sauce from Step 4, and mix well until it completely melts and blends in. Season with salt, pepper, and soup stock granules..
  6. Add the ingredients from Step 1 to the frying pan, and thoroughly combine with the white sauce..
  7. Transfer to a heat-resistant dish, swirl in the heavy cream, add cheese on top, and bake in a toaster oven for about 5 minutes..
  8. Sprinkle with panko and dried parsley before baking for a more colorful result..
  9. You can make a stew with this white sauce. Use different ingredients to create your own recipes using this sauce..
