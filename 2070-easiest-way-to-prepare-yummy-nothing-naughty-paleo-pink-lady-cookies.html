Ingredients of nothing naughty paleo pink lady cookies

  1. Prepare 100 grams of almond meal.
  2. You need 50 grams of hazelnut meal.
  3. Prepare 30 grams of flaxseed meal.
  4. You need 2 tbsp of cinnamon.
  5. You need 1 tbsp of all spice.
  6. It's 2 tbsp of coconut flour.
  7. It's 1 pinch of salt.
  8. You need 1 tsp of organic vanilla paste.
  9. It's 130 grams of dates.
  10. It's 2 of eggs.
  11. You need 2 tbsp of coconut oil - melted.
  12. Prepare 2 of pink lady apples with skin on - food processed into little chunks.

nothing naughty paleo pink lady cookies instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180c..
  2. Line two baking trays with grease proof cooking paper..
  3. Combined all dry ingredients in a large bowl and mix well with a wooden spoon..
  4. Core the apples & food process until chunky. Do not over process or the mix will turn to paste..
  5. Food process the dates with eggs, coconut oil & vanilla paste until well mixed. High speed approx 40 sec..
  6. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix well with a wooden spoon..
  7. Roll into small balls & place on the baking trays. NB: It is a sticky dough.
  8. Press lightly with a fork to flatten..
  9. Bake for 13 minutes..
  10. Allow to cool..
