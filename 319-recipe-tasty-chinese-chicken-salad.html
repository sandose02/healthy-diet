Ingredients of Chinese Chicken Salad

  1. It's 250 grams of spaghetti noodles.
  2. You need 2 tbsp of soy sauce.
  3. It's 1 tbsp of rice vinegar.
  4. It's 1 tbsp of sesame oil.
  5. You need 1 tsp of ground ginger.
  6. You need 1 pinch of salt.
  7. You need 1 tsp of sugar.
  8. Prepare 10 oz of cooked skinless chicken breast, shredded.
  9. You need 2 medium of carrots, cut into matchsticks.
  10. Prepare 2 of green onions, finely sliced.
  11. You need 1 medium of red pepper, cut into matchsticks.

Chinese Chicken Salad instructions

  1. bring pot of water to boil. add noodles.cook noodles until al dente. drain and rinse. let cool before adding to cut up veggies..
  2. combine the soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, ground ginger, and salt..
  3. cut carrots and bell pepper into matchsticks..
  4. slice green onions thinly. add to bell peppers..
  5. shed chicken. toss all ingredients together..
