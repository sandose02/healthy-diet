Ingredients of Marilyn's Bran Muffins

  1. Prepare 2 c of bran (round pellet type).
  2. You need 2 c of boiling water.
  3. You need 1 1/4 c of butter.
  4. Prepare 3 c of sugar.
  5. It's 4 of beaten eggs.
  6. Prepare 1 Quart of buttermilk.
  7. It's 5 c of flour.
  8. It's 5 tsp of baking soda.
  9. It's 1 Tbsp of salt.
  10. You need 4 c of bran (long skinny type).

Marilyn's Bran Muffins instructions

  1. Mix bran (round pellet) and boiling water together, stir, set aside.
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar, add eggs, stir in slowly buttermilk. Once mixed together stir in pellet bran (moist).
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, soda, and salt.
  4. Slowly add and stir in the flour mixture to the buttermilk/bran mixture until mixed together. Add the long skinny bran, stir by hand..
