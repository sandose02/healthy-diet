Ingredients of Peanut Butter Cookies

  1. You need 1 1/2 cup of flour.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of peanut butter.
  3. It's 3/4 cup of sugar.
  4. You need 1/4 cup of vegan butter.
  5. It's 1 tsp of vanilla.
  6. You need 1/2 tsp of baking powder.
  7. You need 1 of -2 TB oil.

Peanut Butter Cookies step by step

  1. preheat oven to 400°.
  2. Mix peanut butter and butter.
  3. add sugar, vanilla.
  4. sift flour and baking powder and mix well.
  5. add oil to make dough.
  6. roll 2 inch balls, press with fork on ungreased cookie sheet..
  7. bake 10-12 minutes.
