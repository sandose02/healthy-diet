Paleo Chicken Fajitas with Guacamole step by step

  1. In medium bowl, combine garlic, cumin, oregano, chili powder, sea salt. Toss chicken in mixture until fully coated and set aside..
  2. Heat a large saute pan over medium-high heat. When pan is hot, add coconut oil..
  3. Saute onion for three minutes. Add chicken and continue to cook until chicken is almost fully cooked (about 10-15 minutes), stirring often..
  4. Just before chicken is done, add red peppers, lemon and lime juice. Stir and cook for 3 minutes..
  5. Serve over lettuce and top with guacamole and salsa..
