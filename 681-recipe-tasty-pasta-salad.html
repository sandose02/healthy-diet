Pasta Salad.

You can have Pasta Salad using 15 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Pasta Salad

  1. It's 1 (16 oz.) of Box Rotini pasta.
  2. It's 1 head of broccoli florets.
  3. You need 1 pack of grape tomatoes halved.
  4. Prepare 1 of cucumber, seeds removed, diced fine.
  5. You need 1 of bell pepper diced.
  6. It's of Green onions sliced.
  7. You need 1 can of large black pitted olives, sliced.
  8. It's 1 of bottle, about 10 greek peppers, whole (peppercinis).
  9. Prepare 1 block of colby cheese cubed.
  10. Prepare 1 block of pepper jack cheese cubed.
  11. Prepare 1 of ham steak cubed.
  12. Prepare 1 bag of mini pepperonis.
  13. It's 1 packet of Good Seasons Italian seasoning mix.
  14. Prepare Pinch of white sugar.
  15. It's 1 (24 oz.) of bottle olive garden creamy Italian dressing.

Pasta Salad step by step

  1. Boil pasta according to directions and drain then run cold water over pasta to cool, drain again and set aside.
  2. Put together entire ingredients into large mixing bowl (except the pasta and bottle dressing) turning to mix.
  3. Add pasta and bottle dressing and turn in bowl with spatula until evenly coated, I also add some juice from the pepper jar.
  4. Cover and place in fridge...at least 2-3 hours to marry the flavors Overnight is better.
