Vickys Magic Wands/Fairy Wands/Sparklers! Kids Party Idea instructions

  1. Melt the chocolate in the microwave stirring after every 30 seconds until done.
  2. Let the chocolate cool a little and meanwhile, line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Dip one end of a breadstick into the melted chocolate, twirling to shake off the excess, then repeat on the other end.
  4. Lay the breadstick on the lined tray and repeat for the rest of the breadsticks.
  5. Shake the sprinkles over and put the tray in the fridge a while to set.
  6. If you get bored of dipping breadsticks you can dip Ritz! My oldest son prefers these lol (not gluten-free).
  7. Dip thick banana slices and freeze for half an hour. Delicious!.
