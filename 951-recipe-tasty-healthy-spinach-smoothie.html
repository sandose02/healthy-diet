Ingredients of Healthy Spinach Smoothie

  1. It's 1 cup of clean spinach leaves.
  2. Prepare 1 of chilled and sliced very large ripe banana.
  3. It's 1-2 tbsp of nuts of your choice.
  4. It's 1 cup of cold soy/almond or low fat milk.
  5. You need 1 teaspoon of honey optional.

Healthy Spinach Smoothie step by step

  3. Serve immediately or serve chilled.
