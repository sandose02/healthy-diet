Ingredients of Greek Salad

  1. You need 2 oz of feta cheese, crumbled.
  2. It's 1/4 c of fresh dill, chopped.
  3. It's 1/4 c of olive oil.
  4. It's 2 T of red wine vinegar.
  5. Prepare of juice of one lemon.
  6. You need 1-1/2 tsp of dried oregano.
  7. You need 1 tsp of salt, plus more to taste.
  8. It's 1 tsp of black pepper, plus more to taste.
  9. Prepare 1 of cucumber, halved and sliced.
  10. You need 1 of large green bell pepper, chopped.
  11. It's 1 pint of grape or cherry tomatoes, halved.
  12. You need 1 of small red onion, halved and sliced, soaked in ice water 15 min. and drained.
  13. It's 1/3 c of black olives.
  14. You need 12 of pepperoncini, optional.

Greek Salad instructions

  1. In large bowl, whisk together feta, dill, oil, vinegar, juice, oregano, salt and pepper..
  2. Stir in cucumbers, green peppers, tomatoes, onion and pepperoni. Serve immediately..
