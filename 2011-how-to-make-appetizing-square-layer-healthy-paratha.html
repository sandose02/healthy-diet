Square layer healthy paratha instructions

  1. First of all take a pan an add all the vegetables and half teaspoon of salt to remove it's all moisture. and cook it for 1 minute. In a bowl take one cup of maida and two cup of wheat flour add at the cook mixture of vegetables. then add the dry ingredients that is garam masala powder, amchoor powder, jeera powder Dhaniya powder and then 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste, green chilli paste and add salt as per taste..
  2. After adding all these ingredients put slowly slowly water in it. And make a Atta dough. Put some oil on dough and knead it properly..
  3. Now take a small portion of dough and make a round paratha. apply some oil on surface Sprinkle flour on surface. Fold one side of paratha and another side on it. now again fold one side of paratha on the other side and now roll it in a square shape..
  4. Now place the paratha on pan. Fry the paratha until it get crispy from both side. now paratha is ready and serve it with tomato chutney, green chilli chutney, dahi or achar it taste very good with this all..
