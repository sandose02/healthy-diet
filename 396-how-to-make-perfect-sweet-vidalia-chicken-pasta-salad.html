Ingredients of Sweet vidalia chicken pasta salad

  1. It's 1/2 box of rotini tri color pasta.
  2. Prepare 2 of boneless, skinless chicken breast.
  3. You need 1/2 tsp of garlic powder.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of chicken broth.
  5. It's 1 of salt and pepper.
  6. It's 1/2 of red pepper, diced.
  7. You need 1/2 cup of green onions, chopped.
  8. You need 1/2 tsp of paprika.
  9. It's 1 tbsp of sweet relish.
  10. You need 1 cup of Sweet vidalia onion salad dressing.

Sweet vidalia chicken pasta salad step by step

  1. cook the pasta according to box directions.
  2. place chicken into a pan, add broth, garlic, season with salt and pepper. cook for 30 minutes on medium low heat, turning once. precooked chicken can be used, such as canned or rotisserie..
  3. once chicken is cooked, cooled, and diced, add to the pasta along with the red pepper and green onion..
  4. add in the paprika, relish, and salad dressing. more or less of these ingredients can be used to your liking. other veggies and meats can be used as well. enjoy!!.
