  4. It's 1/2 cup of fresh orange juice.

  6. You need 4-6 of ice cubes.

Strawberry mango smoothie step by step

  1. Wash and cut strawberries.
  2. Cut mango into slices.
  3. Add all the ingredients to jar of blender. Start at low speed and then increase speed to blend smooth..
  4. Add ice cubes and again blend it. Do not completely crush the ice cubes..
  5. Pour into glasses and ENJOY!!!!.
