Ingredients of Chinese Chicken Salad Dressing

  1. You need 1/4 cup of soy sauce.
  2. Prepare 2.5 Tablespoons of sugar.
  3. You need 2 Tablespoons of water.
  4. Prepare 1.5 Tablespoons of distilled white vinegar.
  5. Prepare 2 Tablespoons of minced shallot or onion.
  6. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder or 1/2 teaspoon grated fresh garlic.
  7. It's 1/8 teaspoon of ginger powder or 1/4 teaspoon grated fresh ginger.
  8. It's 1.5 Tablespoons of vegetable oil.
  9. Prepare 1 Tablespoon of toasted sesame oil.
