Ingredients of Black Tobiko Pasta Salad

  1. It's of Black Tobiko (Fish Egg).
  2. Prepare of Pasta (Rotini Suggested).
  3. Prepare of Baby Corn (Canned).
  4. It's of Black Olive.
  5. You need of Cherry Tomato.
  6. It's of Corn.
  7. It's of Japanese Mayonnaise.
  8. Prepare of Olive Oil.
  9. Prepare of Soy Sauce.
  10. You need of Lemon Juice.
  11. You need of Salt and Pepper.
  12. You need of Butter lettuce.

Black Tobiko Pasta Salad instructions

  1. Cook pasta "Al Dente"!.
  2. Prepare dressing. Mix 1/4 cup of olive oil, 1/4 cup of mayonnaise, 2 tbsp of soy sauce, 2 tbsp of lemon juice, dash of salt & pepper..
  3. Boil canned baby corn and corn..
  4. Cut cherry tomatoes and black olives into halves..
  5. Cut baby corn into 1 inches of size..
  6. Spread butter lettuce leaves on the plate..
  7. Mix all the ingredients with dressing and place it on the plate over butter lettuce..
