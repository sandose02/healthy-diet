Vickys Cheese Straws, GF DF EF SF NF step by step

  1. Preheat the oven to gas 8 / 230C / 450Â°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix the flour and cayenne together.
  3. Pulse the butter and flour together in a food processor until crumbly as if making pastry. Add some ice water if the dough needs help coming together.
  4. Tip onto a lightly floured surface and knead the cheeses into the dough.
  5. Break even-sized pieces of the dough off and roll by hand into a straw shape.
  6. Place on the baking sheet and bake for around 6 - 8 minutes or until lightly golden.
  7. You can form balls and flatten them too. Give them an extra few minutes to bake.
  8. Let cool on a wire rack once firmed up enough to move them.
