Ingredients of Chicken Waldorf Salad

  1. You need 3 cups of cooked diced chicken.
  2. You need 2 cups of grapes, halved.
  3. Prepare 2 of apples, diced.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of walnuts, chopped.
  5. You need 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt.
  6. It's 1/2 cup of mayonnaise.
  7. Prepare 10 cups of fresh mixed greens.

Chicken Waldorf Salad instructions

  1. Cook and dice chicken..
  2. Cut grapes and add to bowl..
  3. Cut apples and add to bowl..
  4. Chop walnuts and add to bowl..
  5. In separate bowl, combine Greek yogurt and mayonnaise..
  6. Mix into bowl with chicken, apples, grapes and walnuts..
  7. Enjoy served with fresh greens!.
  8. This is a great lunch for meal prepping. Just place salad in separate bag to keep from getting soggy..
