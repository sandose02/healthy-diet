Ingredients of Vegan sugar cookies

  1. It's 2 cups of all purpose flour.
  2. It's 1 tsp of baking soda.
  3. It's 1/2 tsp of salt.
  4. You need pinch of cinnamon.
  5. It's 3/4 cup of sugar.
  6. It's 1/2 cup of vegan butter.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of vanilla essence.
  8. You need 1/2 tsp of coconut essence.
  9. It's 1 tbsp of sprinkles.
  10. It's 2 tbsp of soya milk.

Vegan sugar cookies step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 180 deg celc.
  2. Mix butter and sugar till creamy, then add essences.
  3. Mix all dry ingredients.
  4. Add dry ingredients to sugar butter and mix.
  5. Add soya milk.
  6. Batter shouldnt be too wet, just enough to pinch together.
  7. Roll balls and put on a lined baking sheet then flatten.
  8. Bake for 10-15mins.
  9. Decorate with icing sugar and sprinkles once cooled.
