Healthy vegan tutti fruity cake crumb cookies step by step

  1. First of all, prepare your flax egg. Mix the ground flaxseed with the 6 tablespoons of water in a small dish and set aside to thicken..
  2. Put the leftover cake crumbs in a bowl and use a fork to break the crumbs..
  3. Add the Jaggery powder,Oats, wheat flour,baking powder and salt to the breadcrumbs, mix together..
  4. Make a well in the centre, add the prepared flax egg and the remaining ingredients. Stir until well combined and a thick, smooth, shiny dough has formed..
  5. Place the mixture in the fridge for 30 minutes to firm up. Meanwhile, preheat the Airfryer to 180Â°C for 2 minutes..
  6. After about 30 minutes, remove the cookie dough from the fridge. Divide into about 12-13 portions. Roll each into a ball and flatten a little in your palms until it's about 2-3cm thick..
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes now on 160*.Once done allow it to cool.Relish it with your hot cuppa..
