Ingredients of Asian teriyaki lettuce wraps

  1. Prepare 1 1/2 lb of boneless skinless chicken breasts.
  2. You need 3/4 cup of mushrooms.
  3. Prepare 1 can of water chestnuts (drained).
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of ground ginger.
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp of rice vinegar.
  6. It's 2 tbsp of low sodium teriyaki sauce.
  7. You need 1 tbsp of low sodium soy sauce.
  8. You need 1/2 tsp of garlic powder.
  9. It's 1/2 cup of green onions.
  10. You need 1/2 cup of shredded carrots.
  11. Prepare 1/3 cup of sliced almonds.
  12. Prepare 2 tbsp of pure sesame seed oil (divided).
  13. You need 1 packages of romain hearts lettuce.

Asian teriyaki lettuce wraps instructions

  1. Heat 1tablespoon of sesame seed oil in skillet.
  2. Add chicken and cook for 3minutes or until Brown.
  3. Add water chestnuts and mushrooms and ginger to chicken mixture, stir and let cook for another 5minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.
  4. Add rice vinegar, teriyaki sauce, soy sauce, garlic powder remainder of sesame seed oil and crushed red pepper to mixture and stir while still heating on fire.
  5. Top mixture with carrots,onions and almonds.
  6. Spoon onto romain hearts and enjoy.
