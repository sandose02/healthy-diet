Green Stuff (or Pistachio Dessert) step by step

  1. Drain can of crushed pineapple and put in large mixing bowl..
  2. Add pistachio instant pudding. Mix well with spoon..
  3. Add cottage cheese. Mix well with spoon..
  4. Add cool whip. Mix well with spoon..
  5. Cover and chill in refridgerator for 2-4 hours..
  6. Serve and enjoy!.
