Ingredients of Ultimate Green Blender Smoothie

  1. You need 10 oz of Fresh spinach.
  2. It's 1/3 bunch of Fresh cilantro (with some stem).
  3. You need 1 of Fresh mango - peeled and cut.
  4. It's 1 of Apple - cored and cut with peel on.
  5. Prepare 1 of Banana.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of Fresh pineapple.
  7. You need 2 tbsp of Lemon juice.
  8. Prepare 1 cup of Cranberry juice (100% juice).
  9. Prepare 1 of Airtight 3 quart container.

Ultimate Green Blender Smoothie step by step

  1. With about 1 cup water, blend 1/2 spinach. Add mango and pineapple as blend further. Pour mixture into container..
  2. With more water or cranberry juice, blend rest of spinach. Add banana and lemon juice and blend further. Add mixture to container..
  3. Adding the juice if not used or about 1/2 cup water, blend cilantro and apple. Pour mixture into container..
  4. Shake or stir contents. Makes approximately 5 24oz servings. Refrigerate unused portions..
