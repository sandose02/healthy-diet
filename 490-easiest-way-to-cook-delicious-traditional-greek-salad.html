Ingredients of Traditional Greek Salad

  1. It's 1/2 of cucumber, cubed (peeled and seeded).

  4. It's 2 oz of Greek olives, sliced.
  5. It's 1/8 cup of feta cheese, cubed (or crumbled).
  6. Prepare 1/8 cup of olive oil.
  7. You need 1 tbs of dried oregano.
  8. It's 1/2 tsp of crushed red pepper.
  9. It's 1/2 tsp of granulated garlic (or minced).
  10. It's to taste of Salt & Pepper,.

Traditional Greek Salad step by step

  1. Combine all ingredients and mix well. Refrigerate for 30-45 mins before serving. ENJOY!.
