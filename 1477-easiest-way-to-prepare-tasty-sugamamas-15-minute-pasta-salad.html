  2. Prepare 1/4 cup of Virgin Olive Oil.
  3. You need 1 of large Tomato.
  4. It's 1 stalk of Celery.
  5. It's 1 of small Red Onion.

  10. It's 1 1/2 cup of Bell peppers, finely chopped (red, yellow, orange).

  12. You need 4 dash of Salt.
  13. You need 6 dash of Salad Seasoning.
  14. It's 3 dash of Dried Rosemary.
  15. Prepare 6 dash of Italian Seasoning.
  16. Prepare 1 of small Lemon.
  17. It's 3 cup of Italian Salad Dressing.
  18. It's of Optional Ingredients For Pasta Salad.
  19. Prepare 2 cup of Hard Salami, pre-cut. Cut circles in half.
  20. It's 2 cup of banana peppers or pepperoncinis.
  21. It's 1 cup of pickled artichokes, chopped.

  23. It's 1 cup of broccoli, cooked.

SugaMamas 15 Minute Pasta Salad! step by step

  1. Boil pasta with olive oil for approx 10 - 15 mins.
  2. Finely slice all vegetables and put in large bowl.
  3. Add 1/2 of all spices to vegetables..
  4. Add 1 Cup of Italian dressing to vegetables. Mix /Toss ingredients..
  5. Drain pasta. Add to vegetables. Add rest of seasoning and 1 - 2 Cups of Italian dressing (to taste). Squeeze some lemon juice on top. Mix /Toss til coated well with Italian dressing and seasonings. Refrigerate for about an hour..
