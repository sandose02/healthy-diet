Ingredients of Ramen Coleslaw

  1. Prepare 2 tbsp of Vegetable oil.
  2. It's 3 tbsp of White wine vinegar.
  3. It's 2 tbsp of White sugar.
  4. It's 3 oz of Package chicken ramen noodles.
  5. You need 1 tsp of Salt.
  6. You need 1 tsp of Pepper.
  7. It's 1/2 head of Red Cabbage (Shredded).
  8. You need 5 of Green Onions (Chopped).
  9. It's 1 dash of Sesame seeds (Optional).
  10. Prepare 1 dash of Chopped almonds (Optional).

Ramen Coleslaw instructions

  1. Remove chicken seasoning from ramen noodles and crush the ramen..
  2. Shred the cabbage..
  3. Mix the vegetable oil, white wine vinegar, white sugar, ramen noodle chicken seasoning, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl..
  4. Toss dressing with shredded cabbage, crushed ramen, and chopped green onions..
  5. Mix well and serve. (Best served refrigerated).
  6. Top with toasted sesame seeds and chopped almonds if desired..
