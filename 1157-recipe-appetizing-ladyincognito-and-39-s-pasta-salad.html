Ingredients of LadyIncognito's Pasta Salad

  1. It's 1 Box of Tri color rotini.
  2. Prepare 1 of small red onion diced.
  3. You need 1 can of artichoke hearts quartered.
  4. Prepare Handful of grape tomatoes cut into thirds.
  5. You need 1 of roma tomato diced.
  6. Prepare 1 of pickle sliced (I used my homemade pickles).
  7. Prepare 6 ounces of snap peas.
  8. It's Bottle of Italian dressing.
  9. Prepare Pinch of black pepper or red pepper flakes for a kick.

LadyIncognito's Pasta Salad step by step

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions..
  2. Using a large mixing bowl add all other ingredients except the Italian dressing while waiting on pasta to cook..
  3. Drain pasta and add to bowl. Add dressing in increments so as to not drown mix. Mix well. Can be served immediately or chilled..
  4. P.s. For those who don't like the raw onion taste you can add it to the pasta during the last 3 minutes to blanch it some..
  5. Enjoy..
