Ingredients of Vegan chocolate chip oatmeal cookies with rasped coconut

  1. You need 150 grams of Butter.
  2. It's 75 grams of Brown sugar.
  3. It's 50 grams of Rasped coconut.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of Agave syrup.
  5. You need 1 tsp of Salt.
  6. You need 250 grams of Oatmeal.
  7. You need 100 grams of Whole wheat flour.
  8. Prepare 50 grams of Ricemilk chocolate.
  9. You need 5 of Walnuts.

Vegan chocolate chip oatmeal cookies with rasped coconut step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 160 celcius.
  2. Melt butter.
  3. Add sugar, cocos, salt, agave syrup and walnuts (cut up first).
  4. Mix in oatmeal.
  5. Mix in flour.
  6. Cut up chocolate and mix in the chocolate chips.
  7. Optional: add 1 or 2 tbsp of peanut butter.
  8. Make cookies and put them in the oven for about 20-25 minutes.
  9. Let cool.
