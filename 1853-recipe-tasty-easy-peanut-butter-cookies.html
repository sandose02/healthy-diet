Ingredients of easy peanut butter cookies

  1. It's 1 can of 14 oz sweetened condensed milk . low fat.
  2. It's 1 cup of peanut butter.
  3. You need 1 each of egg.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of vanilla.
  5. Prepare 2 cup of bisquick or similar baking mix.
  6. You need 1 each of sugar.

easy peanut butter cookies step by step

  1. beat milk, peanut butter, egg and vanilla until smooth.
  2. add bisquick and mix well.
  3. chill at least 1 hour.
  4. roll into 1 inch balls and roll in sugar.
  5. place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet and fglatten with fork.
  6. bake 6 -8 minutes at 350.
  7. cool and store tightly covered at room temp.
  8. makes about 5 dozen.
