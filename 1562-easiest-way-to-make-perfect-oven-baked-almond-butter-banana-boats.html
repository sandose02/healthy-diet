Oven-Baked Almond Butter Banana Boats instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. Using a butter knife, cut about 1/2 inch deep down the length of each banana. With the back of a spoon, widen the cut to make room for the almond butter..
  3. Spoon the almond butter throughout the opening in the banana. Sprinkle with cinnamon. And wrap completely in aluminum foil..
  4. Bake for 10-15 mins. The older/softer/smaller the banana, the less time it needs..
  5. Remove from oven and let cool for 1-2 mins.
  6. Carefully unwrap and enjoy!.
