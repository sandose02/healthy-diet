Vegan chocolate ice cream step by step

  1. Peel, and cut bananas into small pieces..
  2. Keep them in deep freeze for few hours..
  3. Take them out, blend thoroughly.
  4. Add cocoa powder and honey, blend again..
  5. Put the blended content in deep freeze again for about 30 minutes..
  6. Scoop, serve and enjoy.
