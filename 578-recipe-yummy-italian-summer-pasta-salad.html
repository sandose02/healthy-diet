Ingredients of Italian summer pasta salad

  1. Prepare 1/2 of of a cucumber cut into chunks.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of green pepper diced.
  3. You need 1 cup of grape tomatoes sliced in 3.
  4. It's Handful of pepperoni sliced in 4.
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup of chedder cheese shredded.
  6. It's 3 cups of pasta.
  7. It's 4 tbs of apple cider vinegar.
  8. Prepare 2 tbs of olive oil.
  9. Prepare 2 tsp of mccormick salad supreme seasoning.

Italian summer pasta salad step by step

  1. Boil pasta til al dente..
  2. Rinse pasta with cold water and add oil and stir..
  3. Cut up vegatables, pepperoni and add cheese to a bowl. Add vinegar and seasoning and mix well..
  4. Add pasta once cool. And enjoy. For better flavor let in sit over night in fridge so the flavor sets in..
