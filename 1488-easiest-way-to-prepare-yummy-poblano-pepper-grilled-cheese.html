Poblano Pepper Grilled Cheese.

You can have Poblano Pepper Grilled Cheese using 9 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Poblano Pepper Grilled Cheese

  1. You need 4 of poblano peppers.
  2. It's 1 of 14-oz can pinto beans.
  3. You need 3 tbsp of prepared salsa.
  4. It's 1 pinch of salt.
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup of shredded Monterey Jack or Cheddar cheese.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of low-fat plain yogurt.
  7. It's 3 of scallions, sliced.
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp of chopped fresh cilantro.
  9. Prepare 8 slice of sourdough bread.

Poblano Pepper Grilled Cheese instructions

  1. Saute pepper in a bit of oil until tender or you can also cook on grill..
  2. Meanwhile, combine beans, salsa and salt in a medium bowl. Mash the beans with a fork until they begin to form a paste (some can remain whole). Combine cheese, yogurt, scallions and cilantro in a small bowl..
  3. When the peppers are cool enough to handle, slice each one in half lengthwise and remove the stem and seeds..
  4. Heat a panini maker to high..
  5. Spread 1/3 cup of the bean mixture on each of 4 slices of bread. Top with a heaping tablespoon of the cheese mixture. Place 2 pepper halves over the cheese. Cover with the remaining slices of bread..
  6. Grill the sandwiches in the panini maker until golden brown, about 4 minutes. Cut in half and serve immediately..
