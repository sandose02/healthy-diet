Orange cookies (vegan) instructions

  1. Mix all the ingredients and form a dough. And keep it in the refrigerator for 15-20 minutes..
  2. Preheat the oven at 160 -170°C for 10 minutes. Take out the dough and roll it out on a floured surface and make designs of your choice or keep it simple. I have used a stencil to emboss the cookies..
  3. Cut the cookies in desired shapes. Now place the cookies on a lined baking tray and bake for 15-20 minutes checking in between..
  4. Enjoy with tea or coffee..
