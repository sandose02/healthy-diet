Strawberry marshmallow chocolate bites instructions

  1. On a tray lined with baking paper, place the marshmallows, add a thin layer of peanut butter, then place a half of strawberry on top..
  2. While using a spoon, drizzle the melted chocolate over the stacked marshmallows and strawberries to cover..
  3. Place in the fridge to 2 hours harden..
  4. Remove from fridge. Using a piping bag, pipe 6 small dots on top of each chocolate bites. Serve immediately. Return to fridge when not serving..
