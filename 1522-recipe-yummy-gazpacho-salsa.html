Ingredients of Gazpacho Salsa

  1. It's 6 large of vine tomatoes.
  2. Prepare 3 of Bell peppers.
  3. You need 4 of Cucumbers.
  4. Prepare 1 of Red onion.
  5. Prepare 6 of Garlic cloves.
  6. It's 1 cup of Olive oil.
  7. You need 1/2 cup of White wine vinegar.
  8. It's 1 tbsp of Sea salt.
  9. It's 2 tsp of Black pepper.
  10. You need 4 cup of Tomato juice.
