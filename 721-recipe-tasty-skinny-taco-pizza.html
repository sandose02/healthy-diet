Skinny taco pizza instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Spray a skillet with non fat cooking spray.
  3. Add onions, chilles, and jalapeños and brown a few minutes.
  4. Add ground sirloin and cook over medium heat until browned.
  5. Drain excess grease.
  6. Add taco seasoning and 1/4 cup of water. Cook until water is absorbed..
  7. Spray 2 baking sheets with non fat cooking spray and lay flatouts on the sheets.
  8. Put a thin layer of refeied beans on each flat out and top with meat and cheese..
  9. Bake for about 7 minutes or until cheese is melted and flatouts are crispy.
  10. Remove from oven and add tomatoes, lettuce, sour cream and salsa.
