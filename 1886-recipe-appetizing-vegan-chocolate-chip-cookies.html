Ingredients of Vegan chocolate chip cookies

  1. Prepare 50 g of vegan butter.
  2. You need 75 g of light soft brown sugar.
  3. You need 25 g of caster sugar.
  4. It's 100 g of flour.
  5. It's 1/2 tsp of baking powder.
  6. It's 1/2 tsp of cornflour.
  7. Prepare 75 g of dark chocolate.
  8. It's 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  9. It's 1 tbs of dairy free milk.

Vegan chocolate chip cookies step by step

  1. Using an electric mixer, whisk together butter, light soft brown sugar, caster sugar, milk and vanilla extract until soft and lighter in colour.
  2. Sift in flour, cornflour and baking powder. Mix until evenly combined..
  3. Stir in chopped dark chocolate pieces until evenly distributed.
  4. Portion using a tablespoon measure and roll into balls.
  5. Place 4-6 dough balls on your baking sheet with enough space for them to spread..
  6. Bake at 180Â°C for 10-12 mins or until lightly golden around the edge..
