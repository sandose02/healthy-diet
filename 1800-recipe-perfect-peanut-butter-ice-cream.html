Ingredients of Peanut butter ice cream

  1. It's 350 ml of creamy peanut butter.
  2. Prepare 500 ml of low fat curd cheese.
  3. You need 150 grams of chocolate chunks.
  4. Prepare 200 grams of peanuts (unsalted).
  5. Prepare 100 grams of brown sugar.
  6. Prepare 1 of vanilla extract.
  7. You need 1 of crushed ice.

Peanut butter ice cream step by step

  1. Smash peanuts into smaller pieces..
  2. In a blender mix all the ingredients and blend until creamy. Make sure that the chocolate chunks and peanuts are not totally blended so, that there still are some pieces left in the mass.
  3. In freezer safe boxes, place in freezer for about 3-4 hours.
  4. Enjoy :).
