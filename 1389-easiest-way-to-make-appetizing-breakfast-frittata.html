Ingredients of Breakfast frittata

  1. You need 10 large of egg.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of low-fat milk.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of low-fat shredded cheese.
  4. You need 2 tbsp of unsalted butter.
  5. It's 1/2 tsp of salt.
  6. It's 1/2 tsp of pepper.
  7. It's 1/2 cup of mixed colored bell peppers.
  8. It's 1/4 cup of onion.
  9. It's 1/2 packages of ground breakfast sausage.

Breakfast frittata instructions

  1. Brown sausage and drain..
  2. Preheat oven to 350°F..
  3. Mix milk, eggs, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Blend well..
  4. Melt butter and pour into preferred baking pan..
  5. Pour 1/2 egg mixture into pan..
  6. Sprinkle sausage, peppers, onions, and cheese evenly..
  7. Pour remaining egg mixture evenly..
  8. Bake 15-20 mins or till set..
