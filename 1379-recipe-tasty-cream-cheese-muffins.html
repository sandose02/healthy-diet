  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of flour.
  6. It's 1/2 cup of sugar.
  7. Prepare 3 tsp of baking powder.

cream cheese muffins step by step

  1. preheat oven to 400.
  2. beat cream cheese and butter.
  3. add egg and milk and mix well, mix may not be smooth.
  4. combine dry ingrediants and fold in just until moist. batter will be stiff.
  5. fill greased or lined muffin tin about 3/4 full.
  6. bake at 400 about 20 - 25 minutes.
