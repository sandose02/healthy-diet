  2. In a bowl add sliced apple, mango, pomegranate, and green or black grapes. Note. Remove seeds in grapes.
  3. Mix the banana puree into the fruits well.
  4. Serve in a bowl. Put the fruit salad then ice cream as topping. You can add dry nuts also.
