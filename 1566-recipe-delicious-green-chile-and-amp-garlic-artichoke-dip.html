Ingredients of Green Chile & Garlic Artichoke Dip

  1. Prepare 8 oz of cream cheese (room temperature for easier mixing).
  2. It's 10 clove of roasted garlic (mashed).
  3. It's 1/2 cup of mayonnaise.
  4. You need 3 dash of Tabasco.
  5. Prepare 3/4 cup of grated parmesan.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of shredded cheese.
  7. It's 4 oz of diced green chile (preferably hatch or anaheim).
  8. You need 28 oz of artichoke hearts (canned, drained, chopped).
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp of cracked pepper.
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp of lemon juice.

Green Chile & Garlic Artichoke Dip instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350ยบ..
  2. Using a mixer, whip cream cheese until soft and creamy..
  3. Reserve 1/4 cup parmesan cheese and place to the side for later use. Add all remaining ingredients to cream cheese mixture and incorporate well..
  4. Transfer to a shallow oven safe dish and spread evenly. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup parmesan. Bake for 30 minutes..
  5. Once done baking, place dip under broiler for up to 5 minutes or until browned to your liking. Serve with toasted bread or tortilla chips and enjoy!.
