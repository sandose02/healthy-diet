Ingredients of Sig's Prawn and Mandarin Pasta Salad

  1. It's 80 grams of small pasta, I used orzo, but can be other pasta that is used in soups also.
  2. It's 1 1/2 tbsp of thick whisked cream (heavy cream).
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of mayonnaise.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of ketchup.
  5. You need 1 pinch of smoked paprika.
  6. It's 2 of mandarins (or similar fruit), peeled, and cut into smaller pieces , seeds removed, or use the tinned variety but drain well .If using fresh fruit make sure you remove the white pith completly, as it is bitter..
  7. It's 250 grams of large prawns. I used ready cooked ones here..
  8. Prepare 1 of handful of salad greens to garnish.

Sig's Prawn and Mandarin Pasta Salad instructions

  1. First cook your pasta according to instructions on packet, when cooked drain and rinse under cold water , drain and set aside.
  2. Mix the cream, ketchup and mayonnaise and the paprika.
  3. Then mix the pasta under the sauce, it should not be runny.
  4. Place the salad greens on a plate, add the mandarins pieces, then the prawns , spoon the pasta sauce over the top and serve.
