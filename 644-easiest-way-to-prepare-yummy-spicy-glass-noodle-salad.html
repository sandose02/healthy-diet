Spicy Glass noodle salad.

You can cook Spicy Glass noodle salad using 15 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Spicy Glass noodle salad

  1. It's Glass of noodles (as much as you like).
  2. Prepare 8 of long beans cut into 3 cm strips.
  3. It's of Pickled carrots.
  4. You need 250 gms of minced meat (pork/chicken).
  5. It's of lettuce (5 leaves).
  6. Prepare 5 cloves of garlic.
  7. It's 2 tablespoons of dark soya sauce.
  8. It's 2 tablespoons of sesame seed oil.
  9. It's of olive oil.
  10. You need to taste of salt and pepper.
  11. It's half of a torch ginger (bunga kantan) *optional.
  12. You need 3 tablespoon of sambal.
  13. Prepare 1 tablespoon of chilli sauce.
  14. It's 1 of lime.
  15. It's 3 tablespoons of crushed peanuts.

Spicy Glass noodle salad instructions

  1. Minced meat - marinade with salt, pepper and dark soya sauce for half an hour.
  2. Heat olive oil and sesame seed in a pan. Once slightly hot, put in garlic and saute for 2 mins. After that, add the minced meat into the pan. Stir fry till dark and dry. Set aside to cook..
  3. Boil the long beans till slightly soft. Drain and wash with cold water..
  4. Soak the glass noodles in hot water for 10 mins and drain..
  5. Chop the cabbage into slices. Mince the torch ginger..
  6. Add the lettuce, glass noodles, long beans, pickled carrots, torch ginger in a large bowl..
  7. Mix in chilli sauce, sambal, and squeeze in the lime. Mix well till combined..
  8. Add in the minced pork and crushed peanuts..
