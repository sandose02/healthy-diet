Ingredients of Protein packed cheesecake

  1. You need 1 container (24 oz) of Siggis 0% fat Greek yogurt plain.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of Swerve no calorie sugar replacement.
  3. You need 1 cup of Lactaid low fat cottage cheese.
  4. It's 2 scoops of vanilla protein powder.
  5. Prepare 6 tbsp of light soft cream cheese.
  6. Prepare of The juice of 1 lemon.
  7. You need 1 of egg.
  8. Prepare 1 of Quest beyond cinnamon bun cereal bar for each ramekin.
  9. You need 2 scoops of vanilla protein powder.

Protein packed cheesecake step by step

  1. In a blender combine all ingredients till liquid. This will break up the chunks in the cottage cheese.
  2. Crumble up a cereal bar and put on the bottom of a ramekin.
  3. Fill ramekins with mix depending on the size you will get 6-8 cheesecakes.
  4. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  5. Place ramekins in a shallow pan, pour water in pan around ramekins.
  6. Cook cheesecakes for 40-45 minutes edges should be brown.
