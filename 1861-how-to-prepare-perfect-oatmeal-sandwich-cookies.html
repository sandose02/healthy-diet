Oatmeal sandwich cookies step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350, line 2 cookie sheets with parchment.
  2. Stir flour, soda, baking powder, spice, salt & set aside.
  3. Mix dry ing. with oil, sugars, egg, oats, carrots & raisins.
  4. Drop dough by rounded tablespoons 2" apart on preheated cookie sheets.bake 10-12 min until cookies begin to brown.let stand 1 min then remove & cool..
  5. Mix cr.cheese & syrup, spread 1 Tb on flat side of cookie, top with remaining cookies. Wrap & chill up to 3 days..
