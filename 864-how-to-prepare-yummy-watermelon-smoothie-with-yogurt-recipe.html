Watermelon Smoothie with Yogurt Recipe instructions

  1. Remove skin and black seeds from watermelon and cut into large pieces..
  2. Add plain yogurt in a blender jar..
  3. Add watermelon, banana and mint leaves in it..
  4. Blend until smooth puree. Pour it into a serving glass and serve..
