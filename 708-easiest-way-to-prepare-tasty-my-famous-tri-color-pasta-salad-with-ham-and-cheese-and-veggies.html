Ingredients of My Famous Tri Color Pasta salad with Ham and Cheese and Veggies

  1. It's 1 box of of colored pasta.
  2. You need 1 of White cheddar Vermont cheese.
  3. You need 1 of Virginia ham cut into cubes.
  4. You need 1 of Extra virgin olive oil and red wine vinegar.
  5. It's 1 of wishbone Italian dressing.
  6. You need 1 of salt and pepper garlic powder onion powder to taste.
  7. Prepare 1 of floret broccoli frozen or fresh plum tomatoes.

My Famous Tri Color Pasta salad with Ham and Cheese and Veggies step by step

  1. cook tri pasta.
  2. place it in the bowl or baking pan olive oil vinegar salt pepper garlic powder onion powder mix in with Italian dressing.
  3. then combined cube Virginia ham cube cheddar cheese or whatever cheese you would like.
  4. mix everything together let it sit half an hour to an hour and serve and enjoy.
