Ingredients of Red Fruit Salad

  1. Prepare 6 medium of Strawberries, halved.
  2. Prepare 1 bunch of Red Grapes, small bunch.
  3. You need 1/4 cup of Dried Cranberries.
  4. You need 1/4 cup of Salted Cashews (I just used a handful, you can add or subtract to taste).

Red Fruit Salad step by step

  1. Wash and dry fresh fruit.
  2. Cut the crown of the strawberry off and then cut the strawberries in half. (This is not necessary but it looks better and is easier to eat).
  3. Mix strawberries, grapes, dried cranberries, and cashews in bowl..
  4. Drizzle with a small amount of honey and sprinkle with cinnamon and serve.
