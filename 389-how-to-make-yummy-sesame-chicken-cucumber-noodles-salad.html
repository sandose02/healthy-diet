Ingredients of sesame chicken cucumber noodles salad

  1. You need 8 oz of noodles.
  2. It's 1 cup of peanut butter.
  3. Prepare 2 3/4 tbsp of toasted sesame oil.
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup of rice vinegar.
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp of dry sherry.
  6. It's 1 cup of scallions.
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup of cilantro chopped.
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp of soy sauce.
  9. You need 1 tbsp of Asian chile sauce.
  10. It's 2 head of baby romaine.
  11. You need 2 of red peppers.
  12. Prepare 1 of cucumber.
  13. Prepare 1 of toasted sesame seeds.
  14. It's 1 1/2 lb of cooked chicken breasts sliced into 1/4 inch slices and chilled.

sesame chicken cucumber noodles salad instructions

  1. fill large bowl with ice cubes and water.
  2. cook noodles then transfer to ice water.
  3. whisk pb, vinegar, sesame oil, and sherry.
  4. add scallions, cilantro, soy and hot sauce.
  5. cut Romaine in half, notch out core, cut into 1/2 inch pieces.
  6. add lettuce,chicken,peppers,cucumbers.
  7. add dressing and season with salt and water to taste.
