Ingredients of Vegan chocolate chip oatmeal cookies with rasped coconut

  1. Prepare 150 grams of Butter.
  2. Prepare 75 grams of Brown sugar.
  3. You need 50 grams of Rasped coconut.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of Agave syrup.
  5. You need 1 tsp of Salt.
  6. It's 250 grams of Oatmeal.
  7. It's 100 grams of Whole wheat flour.
  8. You need 50 grams of Ricemilk chocolate.
  9. It's 5 of Walnuts.

Vegan chocolate chip oatmeal cookies with rasped coconut instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 160 celcius.
  2. Melt butter.
  3. Add sugar, cocos, salt, agave syrup and walnuts (cut up first).
  4. Mix in oatmeal.
  5. Mix in flour.
  6. Cut up chocolate and mix in the chocolate chips.
  7. Optional: add 1 or 2 tbsp of peanut butter.
  8. Make cookies and put them in the oven for about 20-25 minutes.
  9. Let cool.
