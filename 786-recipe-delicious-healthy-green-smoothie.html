  7. Prepare as required of Fresh grated coconut.
  8. It's 3 to 4 of Banana slices.

Healthy green smoothie instructions

  1. Take a grinder jar,add chopped spinach, chopped kiwi,banana and coconut milk.
  2. Churn it till smooth paste.transfer it in to a bowl and add soaked chiya seeds and mix it properly..
  3. And garnish it with chiya seeds,banana slices, gratedcoconut, kiwi slices and put the cherry in center..
  4. Healthy and tasty green smoothie is ready to serve it's full of fiber and nutrition..
