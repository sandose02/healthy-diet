Healthy Banana Pancakes instructions

  1. Whisk together bananas, cake flour, yogurt, baking powder, and flour..
  2. Whisk egg whites with salt; fold into the banana mixture..
  3. Heat a large nonstick skillet coated with cooking spray over medium heat..
  4. Spoon in 1/3 cup batter for each pancake..
  5. Flip when tops are covered with bubbles and edges are slightly browned (about 2 minutes per side)..
  6. Drizzle with syrup or honey. Enjoy!.
