Ingredients of Low Fat Strawberry Dessert

  1. You need 2 box of 8 oz 1/3 less fat cream cheese.
  2. You need 4 tbsp of Truvia.
  3. It's 1 tsp of Vanilla extract.
  4. It's 1 packages of 8 oz sugar free cool whip.
  5. It's 1 quart of Strawberries.
  6. You need 2 packages of Wick's sugar free glaze ( Walmart has).
  7. Prepare 2 cup of Graham cracker crumbs.
  8. It's 6 tbsp of Butter.

Low Fat Strawberry Dessert step by step

  1. Melt butter and mix into the graham cracker crumbs and cover the bottom of a 13x9 or smaller METAL pan to form crust.
  2. In a separate bowl beat cream cheese ( do not use fat free).
  3. Add Truvia to cream cheese and blend.
  4. Add vanilla and blend in.
  5. Add cool whip and blend in.
  6. Spread this mixture over the graham cracker crust.
  7. In another bowl cut strawberries into 3rds and mix in the 2 packages of Wick's sugar free glaze then spread over cream cheese mixture.
  8. Chill for 4-5 hours then enjoy.
