Vegan light lunch - Tomato Soup + Salad.

You can cook Vegan light lunch - Tomato Soup + Salad using 12 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Vegan light lunch - Tomato Soup + Salad

  1. It's 200 g of tomato purée (or quantity to taste).
  2. You need 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  3. Prepare 1-3 tsp of oregano.
  4. It's to taste of salt.
  5. Prepare 1 of carrot (grated).
  6. You need 1/2 of cucumber.
  7. You need 1/2 of onion.
  8. You need 1 small can of sweet corn.
  9. You need 1 small can of green peas.
  10. It's 5 leaves of lettuce and/or cabbage.
  11. Prepare to taste of (any other vegetable you would like to add, for example tomatoes or lentils).
  12. You need 1 handful of black olives.

Vegan light lunch - Tomato Soup + Salad instructions

  1. For the soup: heat the tomato purée in a small pot on medium heat until warm, add the olive oil, oregano and salt, mix, taste and add salt and oregano to taste. If the purée is too thick, add a few spoonfuls of water, then olive oil, salt and oregano to balance it out. (leaves of basil are also welcome!)..
  2. For the salad: grate the carrot, thinly slice the cabbage and/or lettuce leaves, dice the cucumber, onions. Mix all the remaining vegetables. Add salt, vinegar (or lemon juice) and olive oil to taste..
  3. Enjoy with a piece of toast and orange juice for best experience! :b.
