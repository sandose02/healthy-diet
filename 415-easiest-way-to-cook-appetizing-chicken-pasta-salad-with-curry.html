Chicken Pasta Salad With Curry.

You can have Chicken Pasta Salad With Curry using 11 ingredients and 11 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Chicken Pasta Salad With Curry

  1. It's 2 tbsp of melted butter.
  2. It's 6 of stalks celery, chopped.
  3. You need 2 medium of onions, minced.
  4. It's 1 large of bunch green grapes (optional).
  5. You need 1 lb of rotini pasta, whole wheat or white, cooked and well drained.
  6. It's 2 cup of regular or light mayonnaise.
  7. It's 2 of to 4 tablespoons curry powder, to taste.
  8. It's 1/2 of to 1 teaspoon salt, to taste.
  9. You need 1/2 of to 1 teaspoon garlic powder, to taste.
  10. Prepare 1/2 of to 1 teaspoon pepper, to taste.
  11. You need 2 1/2 of to 3 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts (about 8 breasts).

Chicken Pasta Salad With Curry instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°..
  2. Trim the chicken of any fat..
  3. Place in a baking pan and drizzle with the melted butter..
  4. Season with garlic, pepper and salt..
  5. Bake for about 40 minutes, until cooked through..
  6. Refrigerate until cool and then cut into chunks..
  7. In a serving bowl, combine the chicken, cooked pasta, celery, onion and grapes..
  8. In a separate bowl, combine the curry powder with the mayonnaise and mix well..
  9. Add to the pasta mixture and stir to combine..
  10. Chill for several hours or overnight before serving..
  11. Add extra salt and pepper if desired before serving..
