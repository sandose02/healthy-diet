vegetarian "cold cut" sandwiches. step by step

  1. cut ends off zucchini, using the thinnest blade on a julienne slicer, slice each one lengthwise.
  2. place in container, stir in half a bottle (or to taste) Italian dressing..
  3. let marinate several hours, or over night, in fridge, covered..
  4. serve cold on your bread/bun of choice!.
