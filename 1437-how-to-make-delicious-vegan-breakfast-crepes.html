Vegan Breakfast Crepes step by step

  1. Rinse the spinach leaves well under running water.
  2. Slice them fine and add them to a mixing bowl.
  3. Add rest if he ingredients besides water.
  4. Mix well and then add just enough water to create a medium thick batter.
  5. Heat a pan and drizzle some oil.
  6. Pour a laddleful of the batter on to the center of the pan and spread slightly.
  7. Cook on Low flame till one side is down and flip over to cook the other.
  8. Take off heat once done.
  9. Serve hot with accompaniment of your choice.
