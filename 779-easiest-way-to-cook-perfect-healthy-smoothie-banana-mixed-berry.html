Ingredients of Healthy Smoothie - Banana Mixed Berry

  1. Prepare 1/2 of Plain Greek Yoghurt.
  2. It's 3/4 of Milk.
  3. It's 2 of Bananas, cut in small pieces. (Could be 1 banana, depending on your preference of flavour).
  4. It's 1/2 of Frozen Mixed Berries.
  5. You need 1 Tablespoon of Honey.
  6. It's 1 Teaspoon of oats (optional).
  7. You need of Up to 1 Teaspoon Nutmeg (to taste, optional).

Healthy Smoothie - Banana Mixed Berry step by step

  1. Place Greek Yoghurt, Milk, Banana, Mixed Berries and Oats into a blender. Blend until combined..
  2. Add in the honey and a small pinch of nutmeg. (Not the whole teaspoon). Blend until combined..
  3. Pour into glasses. Sprinkle the nutmeg on top and serve. (Serves 2). Enjoy!!.
