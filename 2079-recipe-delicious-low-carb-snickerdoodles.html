Low-carb Snickerdoodles step by step

  1. Mix together cookie mix ingredients..
  2. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour..
  3. Mix together sugar topping ingredients and store in small dish..
  4. Preheat oven to 350..
  5. Roll dough into small balls and roll in sugar topping mix..
  6. Place on ungreased cookie sheet and bake 10-15 minutes..
  7. Makes approximately 20 cookies..
