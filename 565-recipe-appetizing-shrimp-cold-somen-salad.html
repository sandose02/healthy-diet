Ingredients of Shrimp Cold Somen Salad

  1. You need of For the salad:.
  2. It's 250 gr of shrimps, peeled and deveined.
  3. Prepare 150 gr of broccoli florets, boil in boiling water for 1 minute, remove from the heat, drain.
  4. Prepare 8 of lettuce leaves, cut to taste.
  5. It's 2 bundles of somen noodles, boil in boiling water for 3-4 minutes, remove from the heat, drain in fine-mesh strainer, rinse under very cold water to stop cooking.
  6. It's 2 of julienned carrots.
  7. It's 1 of seeded and julienned cucumber.
  8. You need of For the dressing:.
  9. You need 60 ml of rice vinegar.
  10. You need 50 ml of vegetable oil.
  11. You need 60 ml of Japanese soy sauce.
  12. It's 3 tbsp of granulated sugar.
  13. Prepare 1 tsp of sesame oil.
  14. It's of some toasted sesame.

Shrimp Cold Somen Salad instructions

  1. In a small pot, mix together all the ingredients for the dressing. Boil the dressing over small-high heat until the sugar dissolved. Remove from the heat. Let it cool..
  2. Boil shrimps in the boiling water for 5-8 minutes. Remove from the heat. Drain. Set aside..
  3. Layer lettuce, cooked somen noodles, carrots, cucumber, broccoli, and shrimps. Pour the dressing evenly on the salad. Sprinkle soma toasted sesame on top. Serve immediately..
