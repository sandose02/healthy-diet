CHOCOLATE and FIG LOG instructions

  1. In the food processor, add all the log ingredients and blitz untill just before becoming smooth. Taste. Add more maple syrup/orange juice if needed..
  2. Place the mixture onto a layed out baking paper. Roll into a log. Twist ends and put into the fridge to harden. 1-2 hours..
  3. Remove from fridge. Unwrap. Pour some shredded coconut onto a plate. Roll log through shredded coconut then cut into 1-2cm peices. Serve immediately. Keep in fridge..
