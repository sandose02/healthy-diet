Pizza Puff / Kids Friendly step by step

  1. Mix mozzarella cheese with dried oregano.
  2. You can use pizza sauce or spaghetti sauce.
  3. Gently mix well and put 1 tbsp of cheese and sauce onto puff pastry.
  4. Fold 4 corner upward and squeeze firmly then bake it at 200 C for 15 to 20 minutes.
  5. Loosely cover with foil if tops brown too quickly.
  6. Enjoy as it is.
  7. Serving option.
  8. Add a dash of fine chili and freshly grated parmesan cheese.
