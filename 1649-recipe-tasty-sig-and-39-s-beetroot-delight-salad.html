Ingredients of Sig's Beetroot Delight Salad

  1. It's of salad base.
  2. Prepare 175 grams of of either fresh cooked or prepacked cooked beetroot . Do not waste the juice , it is needed for the dressing. Do not use the pickled kind..
  3. Prepare 1/2 of pomegranate, but you can use as many seeds as you like and catch the juice for dressing.
  4. It's 1 head of sweet, crisp lettuce of choice. Rip the leafs of the stalk and discard stalk.
  5. It's of dressing.
  6. It's 1/4 tsp of paprika powder.
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp of ground ginger.
  8. It's 1/4 tsp of ground cumin.

  11. You need 1/2 of lime, juice only.
  12. It's 3 tsp of olive oil, extra virgin.

  14. It's 1 of good pinch dried tarragon (optional).

  16. You need 1/2 of handful of toasted almonds ( slightly dry toasted in frying pan, or bought ).

Sig's Beetroot Delight Salad instructions

  1. Clean lettuce and get as dry as possible. Take the green leafs of the stalk Discard the stalk, arrange the leafs in a salad bowl. Or arrange on lettuce on side plates..
  2. Top with the chopped beetroot and then the seeds..
  3. Make the dressing , using up the beetroot and pomegranate juice and all dressing ingredients, mix well.
  4. Now add the almonds and nuts sprinkle over the top. Then add the crumbled cheese over the top..
