Chicken-Bacon Caesar Wrap.

You can cook Chicken-Bacon Caesar Wrap using 11 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Chicken-Bacon Caesar Wrap

  1. Prepare 1 packages of Tortillas for wrapping, spinach wraps give a great flavor.
  2. Prepare 1/4 lb of Chicken breast.
  3. It's 1/4 lb of Bacon.
  4. You need 1/4 of Head Iceberg Lettuce.
  5. It's 1/3 cup of Caesar dressing.
  6. It's 1 of Optional: croutons.
  7. You need 1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese.
  8. Prepare of Chicken Seasoning.
  9. It's of salt or Johnny's.
  10. You need of paprika or pepper.
  11. Prepare tbsp of olive oil.

Chicken-Bacon Caesar Wrap instructions

  1. Cook bacon to crispy and crumble..
  2. Either using the same pan with a little bacon grease or vegetable oil, heat up a skillet and cook and season chicken until 165°F..
  3. Chop lettuce up and rinse well..
  4. Add all ingredients into a big bowl and mix well..
  5. Microwave tortillas for 10 seconds. Add a good amount of the mix into a tortilla, fold and enjoy! I tried it with spinach wraps and green leaf lettuce too..
