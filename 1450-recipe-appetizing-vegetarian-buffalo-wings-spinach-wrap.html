Vegetarian buffalo wings spinach wrap instructions

  1. Toast buffalo wings.
  2. In pan, add 2 tbls grapeseed oil or any oil. Add bell peppers, tomatoes, mushrooms, sauteed, add a little salt and ground pepper to taste..
  3. In small bowl, squeeze lime juice, add sugar, ground pepper, white and black sesame, mix well for the sauce..
  4. By now, buffalo wings should be toasted. Place wings in a line on the tortilla, add sauteed vegetables, add sour cream, sesame dressing or sauce on top, add cilantro. Roll by folding both sudes, then roll forward like a burrito..
