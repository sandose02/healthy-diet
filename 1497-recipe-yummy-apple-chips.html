Recipe: Yummy Apple Chips

Healthy Food Recipes For Healthy Diet

Apple Chips.

You can have Apple Chips using 2 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Apple Chips

  1. It's 2 of Granny Smith Apple.
  2. You need 1/2 tsp of Ground cinnamon.

Apple Chips step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 100 fan forced Use an apple corer to take out the core of Apple, (this is optional). Slice the apple as finely as possible otherwise they won't turn out crispy.
  2. Place 2 sheets of greaseproof/baking paper on a baking tray, place the sliced apples on the tray, do not overlap them otherwise they won't crisp..
  3. Lightly sprinkle the cinnamon over the sliced apples. Place in the oven and cook for 1 hour, flipping over at halfway..
