Ingredients of Savory Veggie Muffins

  1. You need of Dry ingredients.

Savory Veggie Muffins step by step

  1. Combine dry ingredients. Set aside..
  2. Stir together ground flax seed with 6 tablespoons water to form flax seed gel..
  3. Combine wet ingredients with flax seed gel..
  4. Saute the onions briefly, then toss in the rest of the vegetables, adding the spinach last. cook 1 minute..
  5. Combine pre-cooked vegetables with wet ingredients. Mix together..
  6. Bring wet/vegetable ingredients and dry ingredients together, just until there is no dry flour left in bowl..
  7. Preheat oven to 400°F. Add batter to muffin pan. Cook for 15-20 minutes..
