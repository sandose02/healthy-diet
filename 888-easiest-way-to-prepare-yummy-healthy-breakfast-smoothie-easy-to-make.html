Ingredients of Healthy breakfast smoothie (easy to make)

  1. You need 1 of banana fresh.
  2. You need 1 cup of frozen mangos.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of frozen pineapples.
  4. Prepare 250 ml of semi skim milk.
  5. It's 3 tbsp of low fat Greek yogurt.

Healthy breakfast smoothie (easy to make) step by step

  1. Add the milk and yogurt to your blender.
  2. Now Brecknock the banana in half and add to the blender.
  3. Add the frozen mangos and pineapples..
  4. Blend for around 5 minutes or until smooth.
  5. Add smooth to a nice glass and relax.
