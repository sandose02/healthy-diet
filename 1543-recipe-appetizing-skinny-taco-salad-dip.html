Ingredients of Skinny Taco Salad dip

  1. Prepare of Appetizers.
  2. It's 1 envelope of taco seasoning.
  3. Prepare 2 cup of lettuce, chopped.
  4. It's 2 cup of tomatoes, diced.
  5. You need 8 oz of sour cream.
  6. Prepare 8 oz of cream cheese.
  7. You need 2 cup of salsa (mild,etc).
  8. Prepare 16 oz of salsa (mild,etc).
  9. Prepare 2 oz of sliced black olives.
  10. It's 2 cup of mont. jack/colby cheese ,shredded.

Skinny Taco Salad dip step by step

  1. Mix sour cream, cream cheese, taco seasoning and salsa well with hand mixer..
  2. Pour cheese,salsa etc. mixture into 9 x 12 dish..
  3. Layer lettuce, tomatoes, shredded cheese and olives (in that order) on top of cheese/salsa mixture..
  4. Serve with chips or crackers of your choice..
  5. Refrigerate leftovers..
