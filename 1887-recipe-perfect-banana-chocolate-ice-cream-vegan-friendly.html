Banana chocolate ice cream:vegan friendly step by step

  1. Peel and cut the bananas into thin PCs.
  2. Place in an air tight tin and freeze for 6 -8 hours..
  3. When bananas are frozen,place in a good,high speed blender and add in the honey,cocoa and almond milk and blend at high speed for 4 minutes.
  4. Remove in an air tight tin and freeze.after 3 hours,whisk the icecream for 3 minutes and freeze again.serve after 4-5 hours of first freeze.garnish with roasted almonds or sprinklers.
