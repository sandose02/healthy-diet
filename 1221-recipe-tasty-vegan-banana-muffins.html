Ingredients of Vegan, banana muffins

  3. You need 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  4. It's 1 tablespoon of peanut butter.
  5. You need 1 cup of flour.
  6. It's Pinch of salt.

Vegan, banana muffins instructions

  1. In a bowl, mash the medium size banana..
  2. Add the milk, I used soy milk..
  3. Now add the vanilla extract and the peanut butter and mix it all together..
  4. Sift 1 cup of flour, one pinch of salt and 1 teaspoon of baking powder..
  5. Now it's time to add whatever you want. I added nuts. You can add almonds or vegan chocolate chips. If you want to add cocoa, use less flourishing.
  6. Mix it all together once again..
  7. Now oil a muffin bowl and put the mixture on it. It should be 3/4 times the height of the pan..
  8. Bake at medium heat for 30 minutes. After 20 minutes, the mixture will start growing..
  9. That's all! Thanks for reading!.
