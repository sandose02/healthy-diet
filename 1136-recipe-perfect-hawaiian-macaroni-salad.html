Ingredients of Hawaiian Macaroni Salad

  1. It's 2-1/2 cup of Hellmans Mayonnaise.
  2. You need 1 pound of elbow macaroni I used corkscrew.
  3. It's 1 cup of shredded carrots I used organic.
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of milk.
  5. Prepare 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar.
  6. It's 2 tablespoons of sugar.
  7. You need To taste of kosher salt and ground black pepper.

Hawaiian Macaroni Salad instructions
