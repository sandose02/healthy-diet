Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 and spray 9x13 with cooking spray.
  2. Prepare pumpkin bars according to package directions.
  3. Add 2/3 cup milk.
  4. Pour into prepared baking dish.
  5. Mix cream cheese, sugar and egg until creamy.
  6. Spread over pumpkin mixture.
  7. Top with crushed walnuts.
  8. Bake for 35 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Cool and Enjoy!.
